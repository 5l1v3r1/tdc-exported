
# Winn-Dixie throws party for Alabama employee on her 100th birthday

Published at: **2019-11-02T18:05:45+00:00**

Author: **Bob D&#39;Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/trending-now/winn-dixie-throws-party-for-alabama-employee-on-her-100th-birthday/1004372639?utm_source=homestream&utm_medium=site_navigation&utm_campaign=homestream_click)

MONTGOMERY, Ala. -
Many people who turn 100 celebrate with a party with family, sometimes at home, other times at nursing homes.
Not Romay Davis. 
Davis, who turned 100 Tuesday, was feted by her employer -- Winn-Dixie, at the Alabama store where she works, AL.com reported.
