The death has occurred of Eileen O'Brien (née McCabe) of Clontarf, Dublin and Castlebellingham, LouthPeacefully at Howth Hill Lodge Nursing Home, Eileen (aged 94 years) beloved wife of the late Vincent and loving mother of Ann and Tony.
