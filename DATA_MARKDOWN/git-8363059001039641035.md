
# The Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse Sequel Is Coming 8 April 2022

Published at: **2019-11-01T22:00:28+00:00**

Author: **James Whitbrook**

Original: [Gizmodo UK](https://www.gizmodo.co.uk/2019/11/the-spider-man-into-the-spider-verse-sequel-is-coming-8-april-2022/)

We knew it was coming. But now we know exactly when: the follow up to the Oscar-winning Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse will hit cinemas in April 2022.
After teasing an announcement last night, the official Twitter account for Into the Spider-Verse confirmed that the sequel for the film is officially releasing on 8 April 2022.
