
# 7 Players Suspended On Soweto Derby Weekend

Published at: **2019-11-07T13:39:00+00:00**

Author: **Soccer Laduma**

Original: [Soccer Laduma](https://www.soccerladuma.co.za/news/articles/local/categories/soweto-derby/the-premier-soccer-league-confirmed-the-seven-suspensions-for-this-weekend/666977)

The Premier Soccer League confirmed the seven players facing suspension, with two Orlando Pirates stars playing no part in the Soweto Derby this weekend.
Mthokozisi Dube and Happy Jele will both watch from the stands this weekend, after the duo received red cards against Kaizer Chiefs and Maritzburg United respectively.

Dube will serve his second and last match suspension against Chiefs this the weekend, while it will be Jele’s first match out.
In addition five more players elsewhere in the Absa Premiership will play no part in this weekend’s action.
Here are the players on suspensions,
Baroka FC - Matome Kgoetyane
Black Leopards - Khuliso Mudau
Chippa United - Tercious Malepe
Maritzburg United - Kwanda Mngonyama
Orlando Pirates – Happy Jele and Mthokozisi Dube
Polokwane City FC - Themba Chauke
