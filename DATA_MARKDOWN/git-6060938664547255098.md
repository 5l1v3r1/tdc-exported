
# Shahid Kapoor’s father Pankaj Kapur feels his son is one of the best actors of his generation

Published at: **2019-11-02T04:52:12+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/shahid-kapoors-father-pankaj-kapur-feels-his-son-is-one-of-the-best-actors-of-his-generation/articleshow/71862210.cms)

Shahid Kapoor is very close to his family, especially his father Pankaj Kapur. The actor shares a special bond with his daddy cool and is often seen seeking advice and guidance from him. Recenty, Pankaj Kapur opened up about son Shahid’s films and his acclaimed performances. The senior actor reportedly feels that his son is wiser than him in terms of his career graph. Shahid became a star first and an actor later and the father feels it was a smarter move.
Speaking about the films of his son, he reputedly added that Shahid is one of the best actors of his generation and that his films like, ‘Haider’, ‘Padmaavat’ and ‘Kabir Singh’ are proof!
Meanwhile, after creating waves with his gripping performance in ‘Kabir Singh’, Shahid is all set to star in yet another remake.
Shahid will next be seen in Gowtam Tinnanuri’s Hindi remake of hit Telugu film, ‘Jersey’. Shahid, who will be essaying the role of a cricketer in the film, has already started prepping up for the movie.
