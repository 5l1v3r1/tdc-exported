
# Wave Hello to your Pokémon with this exclusive Pixel 4 app

Published at: **2019-11-06T13:31:00+00:00**

Author: **Ash**

Original: [MSPoweruser](https://mspoweruser.com/pokemon-wave-hello-pixel-4/)


        Are you a Pixel 4 user? Are you excited for the upcoming release of Pokémon Sword and Shield on November 15th? If you answered yes to both of these questions, then we have the app for you: Pokémon Wave Hello.
      
Exclusive to the Google Pixel 4, Pokémon Wave Hello uses the phone’s Motion Sense technology to let you wave, pet, and interact with your new Pokémon friends.
