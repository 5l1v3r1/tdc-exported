
# YouTuber Compares Original 2007 iPhone to New iPhone 11 Pro

Published at: **2019-11-07T20:24:00+00:00**

Author: **Juli Clover**

Original: [MacRumors](https://www.macrumors.com/2019/11/07/original-iphone-compared-to-iphone-11-pro/)

Popular YouTuber MKBHD today shared a video that compares Apple's original iPhone released in 2007 with the new 2019 iPhone 11 Pro, which was released 12 years later. The video goes over details like camera quality, design, feature set, and more, offering an interesting look at just how much the iPhone has changed over the course of the last decade.
