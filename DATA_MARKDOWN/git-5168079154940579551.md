
# Food outlets in Oman asked to abide by rules

Published at: **2019-11-06T17:39:00+00:00**

Author: **Times News Service**

Original: [Times of Oman](https://timesofoman.com/article/2182904/Oman/Government/Food-outlets-in-Oman-asked-to-abide-by-rules)

Muscat: In an advisory, the Regional Municipalities and Water Resources Ministry has asked all food establishments to abide by the application of Article 85 of Ministerial Decree No. 29/2016 which deals with health requirements related to public health activities.The Ministry emphasized the need to restrict food establishments and food stores for the unavailability of a device to measure the temperature at the store.
