
# One more freighter beached by MATMO

Published at: **2019-11-01T05:05:11+00:00**

Author: **Mikhail Voytenko**

Original: [Maritime Bulletin](http://maritimebulletin.net/2019/11/01/one-more-freighter-beached-by-matmo/)

General cargo ship QUANG VINH 09 was beached by typhoon MATMO at Quy Nhon coast, central Vietnam, in the evening Oct 30, along with 5 other ships, reported by MB yesterday Oct 31.General cargo ship QUANG VINH 09, IMO 8736318, dwt 3073, built 2008, flag Vietnam, manager XUAN LOC TRANSPORT CO LTD.
