Author: **Lebanon 24**

Original: [Lebanon24](https://www.lebanon24.com/news/lebanon-news/642771/lebanon24?src=rss-feeds&utm_campaign=Softimpact-Rss-Feeds&utm_source=SI-Rss-Feeds-Documents&utm_term=Softimpact-Rss-Feeds-Documents&utm_medium=SI-Rss-Feeds-642771)
