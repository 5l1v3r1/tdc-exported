
# Valkyria Chronicles 4 Steam update adds all DLC for free

Published at: **2019-11-07T12:00:00+00:00**

Author: **Chris Moyse**

Original: [Destructoid](https://www.destructoid.com/valkyria-chronicles-4-steam-update-adds-all-dlc-for-free-571776.phtml)

The Steam edition of Sega's tactical RPG Valkyria Chronicles 4 has been updated and is now Valkyria Chronicles 4: Complete Edition.
