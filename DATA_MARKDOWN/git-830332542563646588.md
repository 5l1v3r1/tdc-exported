
# Arizona woman says relatives killed in Mexico

Published at: **2019-11-05T12:39:07+00:00**

Author: **KPHO/KTVK**

Original: [CHANNEL3000](https://www.channel3000.com/news/national-news/arizona-woman-says-relatives-killed-in-mexico/1139220124)

Members of a Mormon community were attacked while traveling near the US-Mexico border, authorities said Tuesday. Nine family members -- all with dual US-Mexican citizenship -- were killed, a relative of the victims said.
