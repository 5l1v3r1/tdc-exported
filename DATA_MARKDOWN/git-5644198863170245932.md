
# Dow, S&P 500 hit record closes as investors digest trade news

Published at: **2019-11-07T20:45:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [CNA](https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/business/dow--s-p-500-hit-record-closes-as-investors-digest-trade-news-12073316)

The Dow and S&P 500 notched record closing highs on Thursday as the latest signs of progress in U.S.-China trade relations relieved investors, but a report raising fresh worries about the outlook for a deal limited the day's gains.
NEW YORK: The Dow and S&P 500 notched record closing highs on Thursday as the latest signs of progress in U.S.-China trade relations relieved investors, but a report raising fresh worries about the outlook for a deal limited the day's gains.
Based on the latest available data, the Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 182.44 points, or 0.66per cent, to 27,675, the S&P 500 gained 8.42 points, or 0.27per cent, to 3,085.2 and the Nasdaq Composite added 23.89 points, or 0.28per cent, to 8,434.52.
