
# Fawad Chaudhry offers India help to control air pollution

Published at: **2019-11-07T11:10:00+00:00**

Author: **https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews**

Original: [Dunya News](https://dunyanews.tv/en/Pakistan/517625-Fawad-Chaudhry-offers-India-help-control-air-pollution)

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Federal Minister for Science and Technology Fawad Chaudhry has on Thursday offered to help India in controlling air pollution by scientific solution, Dunya News reported.
The federal minister took to Twitter and stated that crop burning in Jalandhar is playing havoc with environment on both sides of Punjab border.
Fawad Chaudhry added that Pakistan can help Indian Punjab government to use a machine solution to turn crop waste into a burning billet that can be used as fuel when needed.
