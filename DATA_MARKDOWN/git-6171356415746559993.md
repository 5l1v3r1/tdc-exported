Published at: **2019-11-04T22:17:39+00:00**

Author: **Ryan Whitwam**

Original: [Android Police](https://www.androidpolice.com/2019/11/04/the-nokia-6-2-is-now-available-in-the-us-for-250/)

Around back, the Nokia 6.2 has a slightly more modest 16MP main sensor, 8MP ultra-wide, and 5MP depth (the 7.2 sports a 48MP main sensor).
