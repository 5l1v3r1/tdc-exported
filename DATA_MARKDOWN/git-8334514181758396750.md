
# Cardinal Health Inc wins $2.25 billion U.S. defense contract: Pentagon

Published at: **2019-11-07T22:48:28+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/article/us-cardinal-health-pentagon-idUSKBN1XH2WZ)

WASHINGTON (Reuters) - Cardinal Health 200 LLC, a unit of Cardinal Health Inc (CAH.N), has been awarded a maximum $2.25 billion modification for a contract with the Defense Logistics Agency for ordering and distribution of medical surgical supplies, the Pentagon said on Thursday.
