
# 'Dabangg 3' song 'Naina Lade': After 'Hud Hud', Salman Khan is all set to romance debutant Saiee Manjrekar in this new love ballad

Published at: **2019-11-07T09:02:23+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/dabangg-3-song-naina-lade-after-hud-hud-salman-khan-is-all-set-to-romance-debutant-saiee-manjrekar-in-this-new-love-ballad/articleshow/71953157.cms)

Salman Khan looks quite adamant to get the audience on the edge of their seat as he is making sure that he leaves no stone unturned in promoting his upcoming film 'Dabangg 3'. Taking to social media he shared the link to the audio of his new song 'Naina Lade' with debutant Saiee Manjrekar.
Earlier, in the day reports of Rahat Fateh Ali Khan being replaced by another singer due to the due to differences between the two countries India and Pakistan, had been making rounds on the internet. Now that the love ballad is out, Javed Ali has replaced Rahat and lend his soulful voice to his soothing track.
Announcing the song, Salman wrote, "Yeh hai hamara romantic andaaz... suniye aur maze lijiye with ‘Naina Lade’... #NainaLadeDabangg3" along with the link to the song's audio. While the lyrics suggest us that Salman will be expressing his love to Saiee in this song, fans are eagerly waiting to see their favourite superstar in a romantic avatar.
