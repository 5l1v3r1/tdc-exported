
# Cyclist fatally hit by car in Ipswich

Published at: **2019-11-06T05:05:28+00:00**

Author: **Manoella Macedo**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/cyclist-fatally-hit-by-car-in-ipswich/1005551618?utm_source=homestream&utm_medium=site_navigation&utm_campaign=homestream_click)

IPSWICH, Mass. - A bicyclist died after being struck by an SUV in Ipswich on Tuesday night, police said.
Ipswich Police responded to a 911 call for a bicyclist hit at around 6 p.m on Topsfield Road, at the intersection with Congress Street.
At the scene, first responders found the injured victim, a 58-year-old Ipswich man, and rushed him to Beverly Hospital. He later died from his injuries.
The driver of the car, a 21-year-old Ipswich woman, stayed on scene and cooperated with police.
Authorities have not released the identity of both the driver or the victim.
No charges have been filed against the driver at this time.
Police continue to investigate this incident.
