
# Kelly Clarkson getting her own Las Vegas residency

Published at: **2019-11-04T13:15:34+00:00**

Author: **KTNV Staff**

Original: [Fox 4](https://www.fox4now.com/news/national/kelly-clarkson-getting-her-own-las-vegas-residency)

LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- Kelly Clarkson fans have been waiting for "A Moment Like This."
The Grammy award-winning singer and host of "The Kelly Clarkson Show" (Monday-Friday 2-3 p.m.) announced that she will be headlining a Las Vegas residency called "Kelly Clarkson: Invincible" beginning April 1 at Zappos Theater in Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino.
