
# Cyclone Maha over 1,000 km away from Oman

Published at: **2019-11-01T09:39:00+00:00**

Author: **Times News Service**

Original: [Times of Oman](https://timesofoman.com/article/2154985/Oman/Environment/Cyclone-Maha-over-1000-km-away-from-Oman)

Muscat: Tropical storm Maha is currently around 1,500 km away from the Sultanate's coast the Public Authority for Civil Aviation (PACA) announced.PACA said in a statement online: "The wind speed around the center of the tropical storm Maha is (35-45) knots and it is still classified as a tropical storm with the continued movement in the north / northwest direction. It is currently 1,500 km away off the coast of the Sultanate and no effects are expected in the next five days."
