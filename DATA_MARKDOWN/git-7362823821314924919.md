
# Joy Behar says Elizabeth Warren, others Democrats, should drop out of 2020 race to clear path for Joe Biden

Published at: **2019-11-05T19:53:48+00:00**

Author: **Sam Dorman**

Original: [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/joy-behar-elizabeth-warren-leave-2020)

"The View" co-host Joy Behar argued that Sen. Elizabeth Warren, D-Mass., as well as other 2020 Democratic contenders, should clear the way for former Vice President Joe Biden to grab the party's nomination.
"He has to go," Behar said of President Trump on Tuesday. "Who is going to get him out? And the answer is, at the moment, Joe Biden. So, all the rest of these people are, you know, just really they're just treading water as far as I'm concerned."
When co-host Abby Huntsman asked whether Behar's statement included Warren, Behar said it did.Behar made the comments while discussing Biden's prospects in 2020 and noted that black Americans supported him.
