
# 'Panipat' first poster: Ashutosh Gowariker's period drama starring Arjun Kapoor to release on December 6

Published at: **2019-11-01T06:59:29+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/panipat-first-poster-ashutosh-gowarikers-period-drama-starring-arjun-kapoor-to-release-on-december-6/articleshow/71849398.cms)

Ace director Ashutosh Gowariker is all set to narrate the historical Third Battle of Panipat on the celluloid with his upcoming high-octane period drama 'Panipat'. Starring Arjun Kapoor as Sadashiv Rao Bhau, the film recounts the events that led up to one of the mightiest battles ever fought in the history of India.

Just a while ago, Arjun Kapoor took to his social media handles to share the first poster of the film. The actor announced that the film will hit the screens next month on December 6. Sharing the poster, Arjun wrote, "Come Witness The Battle That Changed History, #Panipat. In Cinemas December 6th."
