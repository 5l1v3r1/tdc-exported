
# Kareena Kapoor unveils ICC Women's T20 World Cup trophy

Published at: **2019-11-01T11:00:05+00:00**

Author: **ANI**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/sports/cricket/news/kareena-kapoor-unveils-icc-womens-t20-world-cup-trophy/articleshow/71851189.cms?utm_source=twitter.com&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=TOIDesktop)

NEW DELHI: Bollywood actor Kareena Kapoor Khan unveiled the trophy of the ICC Women's T20 World Cup in Melbourne on Friday.
"Bollywood star Kareena Kapoor is counting down to the @ICC Women's #T20WorldCup. Who's joining her to fill the @MCG for the final on International Women's Day?" T20 World Cup tweeted.
