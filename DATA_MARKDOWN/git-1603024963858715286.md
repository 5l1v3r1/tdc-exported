
# Thirumalai Chem Consolidated September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 268.60 crore, down 26.08% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-01T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/thirumalai-chem-consolidated-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-268-60-crore-down-26-08-y-o-y-4595191.html)


        Reported Consolidated quarterly numbers for Thirumalai Chemicals are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 268.60 crore in September 2019 down 26.08% from Rs. 363.36 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 9.94 crore in September 2019 down 81.28% from Rs. 53.10 crore in September 2018.

        Close
      
EBITDA stands at Rs. 30.03 crore in September 2019 down 67.4% from Rs. 92.12 crore in September 2018.
Thirumalai Chem EPS has decreased to Rs. 1.62 in September 2019 from Rs. 5.19 in September 2018.
Thirumalai Chem shares closed at 81.30 on October 31, 2019 (NSE) and has given -5.68% returns over the last 6 months and -34.46% over the last 12 months.
