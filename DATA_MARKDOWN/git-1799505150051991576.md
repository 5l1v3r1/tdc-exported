
# Flu Shot Reminders

Published at: **2019-11-06T08:08:31+00:00**

Author: **BRIEF**

Original: [Courthouse News Service](https://www.courthousenews.com/flu-shot-reminders/)

CLEVELAND – A federal court in Ohio ruled in favor of health insurance provider Anthem in a Telephone Consumer Protection Act dispute, because calls made to the consumer, including flu shot reminders, had not been made for monetary gain.
