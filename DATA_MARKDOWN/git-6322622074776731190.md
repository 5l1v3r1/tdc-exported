
# SoftBank Group shares slide over 4% after massive quarterly loss

Published at: **2019-11-07T03:41:56+00:00**

Author: **Reuters**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/softbank-group-shares-slide-over-4-after-massive-quarterly-loss)

TOKYO (Nov 7): Shares of SoftBank Group fell more than 4% in early trade on Thursday after the Japanese conglomerate reported a $6.5 billion quarterly loss, its first such loss in 14 years, dragged down by a massive hit at its giant Vision Fund.
The stock was down 3.4% as of 0022 GMT after shedding as much as 4.2%.
