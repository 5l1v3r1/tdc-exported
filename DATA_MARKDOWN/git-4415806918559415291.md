
# Terminator: Dark Fate disappoints at US box office

Published at: **2019-11-03T17:36:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Breaking News](https://www.breakingnews.ie/world/terminator-dark-fate-disappoints-at-us-box-office-961542.html)

Terminator: Dark Fate has opened well below expectations at the US box office.
Studios estimate the action sequel earned only 29 million US dollars from over 4,000 North American locations.
The film from Paramount Pictures cost a reported 185 million dollars to produce.
