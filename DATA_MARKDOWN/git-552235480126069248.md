
# Man charged in connection with death of woman found in burning car

Published at: **2019-11-07T17:17:52+00:00**

Author: **Jane Hamilton, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/jane-hamilton/**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/news/scottish-news/man-charged-connection-death-woman-20834571)

A man has been arrested and charged in connection with the death of a woman found dying inside a burning car.
Tragic Ann Drummond , 47, was discovered with serious injuries in a field near Bathgate in June this year.
She died in Glasgow Royal Infirmary two days later.
