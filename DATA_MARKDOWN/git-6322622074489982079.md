
# GIC acquires landmark office building in Beijing's emerging financial centre

Published at: **2019-11-07T10:52:53+00:00**

Author: **Uma Devi**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/gic-acquires-landmark-office-building-beijings-emerging-financial-centre)

SINGAPORE (Nov 7): Singapore sovereign wealth fund GIC has reportedly acquired a 50% stake in an office building in Lize Financial Business District (LFBD), an emerging central business district in Beijing, for an undisclosed sum.
The 42-storey Grade A building is situated in a prime location in terms of visibility and accessibility, GIC said in a media release on Thursday.
