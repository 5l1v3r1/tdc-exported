
# Over 1000 without power in Blackrock

Published at: **2019-11-02T20:55:00+00:00**

Author: **David Lynch**

Original: [Dundalk Democrat](https://www.dundalkdemocrat.ie/news/home/490875/over-1000-without-power-in-blackrock.html)

UPDATED: 1700 without power now in Blackrock area.
According to ESB 1200 customers are without power this evening due to an outage.
ESB say the current estimated time of fix is 12.15am.
ESB said this evening: "We apologise for the loss of supply. We are currently working to repair a fault affecting your premises and will restore power as quickly as possible."
