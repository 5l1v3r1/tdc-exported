
# Confirmed: Disha Patani joins Salman Khan for ‘Radhe’ and here’s a glimpse from the film’s muhurat

Published at: **2019-11-01T09:26:58+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/confirmed-disha-patani-joins-salman-khan-for-radhe-and-heres-a-glimpse-from-the-films-muhurat/articleshow/71851665.cms?utm_source=twitter.com&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=TOI)

Salman Khan was scheduled to begin shooting for his cop drama ‘Radhe’ from today and shared a glimpse of the cast from the film's muhurat schedule held in Filmcity Studios today. Disha Patni has joined Salman Khan in this venture, after having romanced the actor in ‘Bharat’, which released earlier this year. ‘Radhe’ will feature Randeep Hooda as the villain and Jackie Shroff has also joined the cast. Directed by Prabhudeva, the film is produced by Sohail Khan and Atul Agnihotri. Salman Khan made the grand announcement on social media.
