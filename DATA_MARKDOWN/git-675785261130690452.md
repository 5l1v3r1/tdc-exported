
# Who won Naoya Inoue vs Nonito Donaire fight? Result from World Boxing Super Series bout

Naoya Inoue beat Nonito Donaire to win the Muhammad Ali Trophy in their World Boxing Super Series fight.
Knockout king Inoue, 26, was putting his IBF crown on the line against veteran champion Donaire, ten years his senior, in Japan.
The pair delivered one of the finest fights of the year as both traded blows until the very final blow.
