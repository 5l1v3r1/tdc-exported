LG is back to the tablet world, two years after the last official product in the United States. The new device, announced in the recent hours in Korea, is the LG G Pad 5 10.1, the tablet with technical specifications far from the latest available in the market.
The G Pad 5 10.1 has a 10.1 inch IPS LCD display with FHD+ resolution (1920Ã—1200 pixels), Qualcomm Snapdragon 821 SoC, whose launch dates back over 3 years ago, 4GB of LPDDR4 RAM and 32GB of storage space (UFS 2.0) expandable via microSD slot.
Finally, the remaining specs include an 8MP rear camera, a single 5MP front camera, sensor for lateral fingerprint recognition, 3.5mm audio jack and 8.200 mAh battery with Quick Charge 3.0 fast charge support via USB type C port.
LG G Pad 5 10.1 is currently introduced only in the South Korean market, in Silver color, at a price not yet announced by the company.
