
# OnMobile Global Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 49.79 crore, down 14.11% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-01T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/onmobile-global-standalone-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-49-79-crore-down-14-11-y-o-y-4594461.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for OnMobile Global are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 49.79 crore in September 2019 down 14.11% from Rs. 57.97 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 4.47 crore in September 2019 down 61.06% from Rs. 11.48 crore in September 2018.

        Close
      
EBITDA stands at Rs. 10.12 crore in September 2019 down 32.53% from Rs. 15.00 crore in September 2018.
OnMobile Global EPS has decreased to Rs. 0.42 in September 2019 from Rs. 1.09 in September 2018.
OnMobile Global shares closed at 34.45 on October 31, 2019 (NSE) and has given -6.00% returns over the last 6 months and -1.57% over the last 12 months.
