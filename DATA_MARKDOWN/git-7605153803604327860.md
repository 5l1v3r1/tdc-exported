Author: **LABAN WANAMBISI**

The warning by the Wafula Chebukati-led commission comes after a section of voters took to social media to post photographs depicting the candidates they voted for after casting their ballots.
According to the IEBS Act, anyone found to be engaged in election malpractice is liable to fines or imprisonment depending on the offense committed.
Two people have so far been arrested over suspicion of voter bribery.
One person was arrested at Mashimoni Squatters polling centers after he was spotted by irate voters dishing out money to voters who were on the queue.
