
# Victim dies following Taunton hit-and-run crash

Published at: **2019-11-07T04:11:55+00:00**

Author: **Manoella Macedo**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/taunton-police-searching-for-driver-in-hit-and-run/1005909694?utm_source=homestream&utm_medium=site_navigation&utm_campaign=homestream_click)

TAUNTON, Mass. - A person was killed in a hit-and-run crash in Taunton on Wednesday evening, the DA's office says.
According to police, officers responded to a 911 call for a pedestrian hit by a car at around 5:30 p.m. in the area of 305 Middleborough Avenue in East Taunton.
At the scene, officers found 85-year-old Jose Ferreira, of Taunton, lying on the road.
