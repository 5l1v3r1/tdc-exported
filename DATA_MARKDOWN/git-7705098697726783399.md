
# Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse Sequel Will Arrive in 2022

Published at: **2019-11-01T21:39:47+00:00**

Author: **Blair Marnell**

Original: [SuperHeroHype](https://www.superherohype.com/movies/470865-spider-man-into-the-spider-verse-sequel-will-arrive-in-2022)

After Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse became a massive success in 2018, a sequel was inevitable. Now, the follow up to the Oscar winner for Best Animated Motion Picture has a date. Sony’s official Spider-Verse twitter account posted a teaser of Miles Morales’ Spider emblem with an inter-dimensional glitch. It also shared the release date for the sequel: April 8, 2022.
