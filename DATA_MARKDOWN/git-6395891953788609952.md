
# Service dog gets her own elementary school photoshoot

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/trending-now/service-dog-gets-her-own-elementary-school-photoshoot/1006041296?utm_source=homestream&utm_medium=site_navigation&utm_campaign=homestream_click)

GRAVETTE, Ark. -
Tosha has a big job to do. She's a service dog for a fifth-grader who has seizures. She accompanies her owner to school every day.
Now Tosha has been immortalized at the Gravette Upper Elementary School after the good doggie sat for her own school photo, KFSM reported.
