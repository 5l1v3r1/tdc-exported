
# Nadal withdraws from Paris Masters before semi-final

Published at: **2019-11-02T15:54:46+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Malaymail](https://www.malaymail.com/news/sports/2019/11/02/nadal-withdraws-from-paris-masters-before-semi-final/1806324)

PARIS, Nov 2 — Rafael Nadal pulled out of the Paris Masters just minutes before the scheduled start of his semi-final against Denis Shapovalov today, leaving the Canadian to play Novak Djokovic in the final.
Second seed Nadal could have secured the year-end world number one ranking by winning the Bercy title this week. — AFP
