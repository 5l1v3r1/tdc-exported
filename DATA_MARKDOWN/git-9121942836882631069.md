
# Bicyclist killed in hit-and-run in Covington

Published at: **2019-11-06T20:50:07+00:00**

Author: **Asia Fields**

Original: [The Seattle Times](https://www.seattletimes.com/seattle-news/crime/bicyclist-killed-in-covington-hit-and-run-collision/)

A bicyclist died after a driver struck him with a car in Covington and drove away Wednesday evening, according to the King County Sheriff’s Office.
The man was struck in the 18200 block of Southeast Wax Road in East King County around 6 p.m.
Collision detectives from the Sheriff’s Office are investigating the hit-and-run. They did not have a description of the vehicle Wednesday night.
