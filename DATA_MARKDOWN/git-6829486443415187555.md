
# The Internet Thinks Kanye West Accidentally Ripped Off ‘The Legend of Zelda’ on ‘Jesus Is King’

Published at: **2019-11-05T21:08:26+00:00**

Author: **Joseph Earp**

Original: [Junkee](https://junkee.com/kanye-west-zelda/227679)

In terms of sonic influences, Kanye West has always claimed to crib from no-one but himself.
That’s not really true, of course: Yeezus, widely considered one of the most subversive of all mainstream rap records, was actually an assemblage of the work being done by other people in the world of underground rap. The whole thing’s an endless Death Grips rip-off — good, but hardly original.
Now, however, the work of an internet super sleuth makes it clear that Kanye might have accidentally nabbed ideas not from another band, but from a video game. And not just any video game, but The Legend of Zelda, one of the most popular titles of the modern era.
The controversy all started when one Twitter user by the name of @everydaylouie discovered that a snippet of the Jesus Is King track ‘Selah’ sounds a lot like the ‘Gerudo Valley’ theme from The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time.
