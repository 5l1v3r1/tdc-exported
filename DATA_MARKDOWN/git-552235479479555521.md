
# Wedding of the Week: Kimberly Brown and David Byars

Published at: **2019-11-06T07:00:00+00:00**

Author: **Leona Greenan, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/leona-greenan/**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/news/local-news/wedding-week-kimberly-brown-david-20766128)

The couple Kimberly, from Motherwell, works as an undertaker at Co-op Funeralcare. David, from Bellshill, works as an HGV tanker driver at Air Products.
When they met Kimberly and David met at Motherwell Point in Motherwell in 2010.
The wedding The ceremony was held at Double Tree by Hilton Strathclyde on September 29, 2019, at 1pm. The reception was attended by many of their close friends and family.
Honeymoon The newlyweds travelled to the Maldives for their honeymoon.
Bridal party Bridesmaids were Alicia, Kadine and Reanna, flower girls were Hazel and Jasmine, best man was Chris and usher was Charles.
Photographer Karen Muircroft.
Flowers Blooms Florist on Ebay.
Cake Marie-Claire.
Hair and make up Katie Malin.
Attire Kimberly wore a dress called Sprinkles by Olivia Grace Bridal and David wore Glencoe Rose by Highland Kilt Connections in Airdrie.
Cars Personally supplied from Ian McFadgen and Co-op Funeralcare.
Thanks Kimberly and David would like to thank all their family and friends that attended the event and made it so special. They very much appreciated the well wishes and gifts they received.
