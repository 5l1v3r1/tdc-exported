
# MetLife working with rescue group to trap famed MNF black cat

Published at: **2019-11-07T16:00:54+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [ABC7 Los Angeles](https://abc7.com/pets-animals/metlife-working-with-rescue-group-to-trap-famed-black-cat/5678328/)

EAST RUTHERFORD, New Jersey -- There is new information about that black cat that stole the show at Monday night's Cowboys-Giants game.MetLife stadium says it brought in a rescue group, PuppyKittyNYCity, to help with the search for the feline.So far, humane traps have been placed throughout the stadium.Officials said on Twitter that despite reports there that the stadium houses and feeds some 300 cats, those claims are "simply not true."However, the Meadowlands Racetrack has a trap, neuter, and release program in place and currently has 30 barn cats.
