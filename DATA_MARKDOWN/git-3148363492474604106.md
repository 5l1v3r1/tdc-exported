
# Harris Proposes to 'Modernize' School Day by Extending Hours to 6 p.m.

Published at: **2019-11-06T15:57:35+00:00**

Author: **Hannah Bleau**

Original: [Breitbart](https://www.breitbart.com/politics/2019/11/06/kamala-harris-proposes-to-modernize-school-day-by-extending-hours-to-6-p-m/)

Harris is reportedly unveiling a bill on Wednesday that would, as Mother Jones describes, “modernize” school hours by extending them to better coincide with the average working hours of adults.
