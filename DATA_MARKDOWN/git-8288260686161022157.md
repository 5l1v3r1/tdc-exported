
# Manhunt after armed gang storm Dudley shop in morning raid

Published at: **2019-11-01T12:45:38+00:00**

Author: **Charlotte Regen, Kelly Ashmore, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/charlotte-regen/, https://www.facebook.com/dudleybclive/**

Original: [birminghammail](https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/black-country/manhunt-after-armed-gang-storm-17184645)

A manhunt is under way after an armed gang stormed a convenience store in Dudley.
The raid took place this morning (Friday, November 1), at around 8.15am, at the One Stop shop on Buffery Road.
Staff were threatened by a gang of three men - armed with knives and crowbars - who snatched the contents of a safe during the terrifying ordeal.
A spokeswoman for West Midlands Police said: "Police are investigating reports of an armed robbery at a One Stop store on Buffery Road, Dudley at 8.15am today (November 1).
"Three men, believed to be armed with knives and crowbars, threatened the staff before stealing the contents of the safe."
Investigations are ongoing and anyone with information can contact the police via Live Chat at  west-midlands.police.uk  between 8am and midnight, call 101 anytime or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
Please quote log number 555 of 01/11.
