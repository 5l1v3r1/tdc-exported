
# Man accused of peeping into bathroom stalls in Long Island Verizon building

Published at: **2019-11-06T15:56:53+00:00**

Author: **Eyewitness News**

Original: [ABC7 New York](https://abc7ny.com/man-arrested-for-allegedly-peeping-into-bathroom-stalls-on-li/5675718/)

GARDEN CITY, Nassau County (WABC) -- A 32-year-old man is facing charges after police say he was caught peeping into bathroom stalls inside a building on Long Island.
Jamali Johnson is facing public lewdness and drug charges.
Police say a woman saw him looking under the stalls in the women's restroom at the Verizon building on Zeckendorf Boulevard in Garden City.
He was arrested at his home on Tuesday.
Detectives request that anyone that feels they have been the victim of such an incident contact Nassau County Crime Stoppers at 1-800-244-TIPS or call 911.
