
# The Outer Worlds will be out on the Nintendo Switch some time in the first three months of 2020.

Published at: **2019-11-07T22:00:35+00:00**

Author: **Stephen Totilo**

Original: [Kotaku UK](https://www.kotaku.co.uk/2019/11/07/the-outer-worlds-will-be-out-on-the-nintendo-switch-some-time-in-the-first-three-months-of-2020)

The Outer Worlds will be out on the Nintendo Switch some time in the first three months of 2020, according to publisher Take Two, narrowing the earlier promise for a release some time in 2020. In an update to investors today, the company also announced the delay of Kerbal Space Program 2 to some time between April 1, 2020 and March 31, 2021.
