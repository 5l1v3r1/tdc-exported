
# TSX rises on energy stocks; Wall Street opens modestly higher

Published at: **2019-11-05T14:47:46+00:00**

Author: **BNN Bloomberg**

Original: [BNN](https://www.bnnbloomberg.ca/tsx-rises-on-energy-stocks-wall-street-opens-modestly-higher-1.1343187)

Canada’s main stock index opened higher on Tuesday, with energy companies among the top gainers for a second straight day.
At 9:35 a.m. ET, the Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index was up 10.70 points, or 0.06 per cent, at 16,680.51.
U.S. stocks opened modestly higher as well. The Dow Jones Industrial Average began up 27.20 points, or 0.10 per cent, to 27,489.31.  The S&P 500 opened 1.23 points higher, or up 0.04 percent, at 3,079.50. The Nasdaq Composite began up 11.03 points, or 0.13 per cent, to 8,444.23.
