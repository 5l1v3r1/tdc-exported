
# IU achieves a certified all-kill with pre-release 'Love Poem'

Published at: **2019-11-02T06:23:00+00:00**

Author: **BijayaMagar**

Original: [allkpop](https://www.allkpop.com/article/2019/11/iu-achieves-certified-all-kill-with-her-new-song-love-poem)

On November 2 at approximately2:30 PM KST, IU achieved a certified all-kill with her newest single, “Love Poem”! 
A certified all-kill l is achieved after a song score #1 on major daily and real-time charts including: Genie, Bugs, Soribada, Melon, as well as Flo. As a result, the song also rises to the #1 spot on the realtime iChart, resulting in a certified all-kill. 
Congratulation to IU!
