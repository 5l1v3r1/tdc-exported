
# New Hampshire couple in their 40's found murdered in Texas

Published at: **2019-11-02T11:17:35+00:00**

Author: **EndPlay**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/new-hampshire-couple-in-their-40-s-found-murdered-in-texas/1004307341)

PADRE ISLAND, Texas - A couple from New Hampshire was found dead in Texas this past week and police said it's a homicide investigation.
The Kleberg County Sheriff's Office in Texas said the bodies of James and Michelle Butler were found on Oct. 28 after the couple was reported missing on Oct. 23 by family and friends.
James, 48, and Michelle, 46, of Rumney, New Hampshire, were last known to have set up their truck and RV trailer on Padre Island in Kleberg County on or around Oct. 15. A friend said they last had contact with the couple on Oct. 16. 
A Kleberg County deputy found what appeared to be human remains on Oct. 27 around 6:45 p.m. on Padre Island. The site was secured and protected overnight, and the following morning officials investigated at the site.
Autopsies revealed the deaths were ruled homicides.
