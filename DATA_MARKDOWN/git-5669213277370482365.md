
# Containers fell overboard in Klaipeda

Published at: **2019-11-06T17:32:22+00:00**

Author: **Erofey Schkvarkin**

Original: [Maritime Bulletin](http://maritimebulletin.net/2019/11/06/containers-fell-overboard-in-klaipeda/)

Two containers fell into water during off loading from container ship FIONA at Klaipeda, Lithuania, on Nov 2, said Klaipeda Stevedoring Company at a meeting, called to explain the accident. According to official statement, there were no hazardous materials in the containers, both containers were recovered.
