
# Vote Suppression

Published at: **2019-11-01T17:21:10+00:00**

Author: **BRIEF**

Original: [Courthouse News Service](https://www.courthousenews.com/vote-suppression-2/)

ALEXANDRIA, Va. — The New Virginia Majority Education Fund, et al. sued the Fairfax County Board of Elections in federal court for not allowing students who live in George Mason University dorms to register to vote because they did not include their dorm room numbers on their applications.
