
# Stormzy calls on Jacob Rees-Mogg to resign over Grenfell remarks

Published at: **2019-11-05T15:36:38+00:00**

Author: **Edward Thicknesse**

Original: [City A.M.](https://www.cityam.com/stormzy-calls-on-jacob-rees-mogg-to-resign-over-grenfell-remarks/)

Grime artist Stormzy has called for Jacob Rees-Mogg to resign after the Tory MP said on Monday that the victims of the Grenfell Tower fire should have ignored the London Fire Brigade’s advice to “stay and wait.”
The rapper lashed out at the leader of the house on Twitter, writing:

“Oi @Jacob_Rees_Mogg you need to resign you’re an actual piece of shit I beg everyone watch this – MP Jacob Rees Mogg in a nutshell saying Grenfell Victims should of had the common sense to escape. I can’t believe the cheek fucking hell these politicians are actual aliens”
