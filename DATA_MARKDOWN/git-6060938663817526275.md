
# Happy Birthday Shah Rukh Khan: Karan Johar, Ayushmann Khurrana and others wish the Baadshah of Bollywood on his special day

Published at: **2019-11-02T03:24:41+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/happy-birthday-shah-rukh-khan-karan-johar-ayushmann-khurrana-and-others-wish-the-baadshah-of-bollywood-on-his-special-day/articleshow/71861557.cms)

Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan turned a year older today and his birthday is nothing less than a festival for his fans. Apart from the traditional gathering at his residence Mannat in Mumbai of his fans, social media has been flooding with messages and wishes for the King Khan. SRK’s Bollywood friends and counterparts too have been taking to their social media handles to wish the actor on his birthday. From his BFF Karan Johar’s heartfelt post on Instagram to his fan Ayushmann Khurrana’s sweet post on twitter, it has been raining wishes for Shah Rukh.
