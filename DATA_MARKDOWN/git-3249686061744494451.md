
# ARMY’s are angry at an Italian singer for allegedly plagiarising BTS’s Fake Love

Published at: **2019-11-07T16:34:00+00:00**

Author: **ratmonster**

Original: [allkpop](https://www.allkpop.com/article/2019/11/armys-are-angry-at-an-italian-singer-for-allegedly-plagiarising-btss-fake-love)

An Italian singer named “Seiell” has recently come under fire by fans of BTS for blatantly ripping off one of the groups most popular songs, “Fake Love”.
His song “Scenne Nennè” was released on October 29th this year. BTS Fake Love was released in May 2018.
