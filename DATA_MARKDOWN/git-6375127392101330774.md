
# P3.4M shabu seized in Zambo

Published at: **2019-11-06T09:37:52+00:00**

Author: **Alfred P. Dalizon**

Original: [Journal Online](https://journal.com.ph/news/provincial/p34m-shabu-seized-zambo)

The official said that the suspects identified as Aljimar Jimla and Alner Andao were placed under arrest during the entrapment operation in Barangay Divisoria in Zamboanga City past 1 p.m. last Sunday.
Aquino said that undercover officers of the PDEA Regional Office 9 and the local police seized 10 plastic sachets containing  around 500 grams of the so-called ‘poor man’s cocaine’ worth P3.4 million and the marked money paid to the duo by a poseur.
A vehicle used by the suspects to transport the drugs was also impounded by the PDEA.
Aquino said that the two suspects are now facing non-bailable charges for sale and possession of dangerous drugs under Republic Act 9165 otherwise known as The Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002.
