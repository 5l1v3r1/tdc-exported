
# Makwabta na ci gaba da guna-guni kan rufe iyakokin Najeriya

Published at: **2019-11-05T20:15:53+00:00**

Author: **Nura Ado Suleiman**

Original: [RFI](http://ha.rfi.fr/najeriya/20191105-rufe-iyakokin-najeriya-ya-haifar-da-ce-ce-kuce-makwabta)

Matakin rufe iyaka da Najeriya ta yi tsakaninta da kasashe masu makotaka da ita, na ci gaba da haddasa ce-ce-kuce a tsakanin jamaâ€™a.Yayin da wasu ke ganin cewa matakin ya dace, wasu kuwa na ganin cewa ya haddasa mummunan sakamako a fagen tattalin arzikin kasar da kuma na makotanta.A Jamhuriyar Nijar, Maradi na matsayin yankin da ke gudanar da hadada-hadakar kasuwanci ta milyoyin kudade tsakaninta da Najeriya, kuma daga Maradi ga rahoton da Salisu Isa ya aiko mana.Rufe iyakokin Najeriya ya haifar da ce-ce-kuce a makwabta 05/11/2019 - Daga Salisu Isah
