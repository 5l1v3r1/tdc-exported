
# Stocks end flat as trade deal concerns weigh

Published at: **2019-11-06T21:06:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [CNA](https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/business/stocks-end-flat-as-trade-deal-concerns-weigh-12069954?utm_source=dlvr.it&utm_medium=twitter)

U.S. stocks slipped on Wednesday following a Reuters report the U.S.-China trade deal could be delayed until December.
NEW YORK: U.S. stocks ended little changed on Wednesday as a report the U.S.-China trade deal could be delayed until December was offset by gains in healthcare shares.
The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 1.29 points, or nearly flat, to 27,491.34, the S&P 500 gained 2.12 points, or 0.07per cent, to 3,076.74 and the Nasdaq Composite dropped 24.05 points, or 0.29per cent, to 8,410.63.
