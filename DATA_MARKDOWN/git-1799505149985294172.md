
# Mining and Manufacturing

Published at: **2019-11-07T02:29:04+00:00**

Author: **BRIEF**

Original: [Courthouse News Service](https://www.courthousenews.com/mining-and-manufacturing/)

ATLANTA – The Eleventh Circuit ruled that the Army Corps of Engineers properly issued a wetland discharge permit to a fertilizer manufacturer that mines phosphate without addressing the environmental effects of a radioactive byproduct of the manufacturing process.
