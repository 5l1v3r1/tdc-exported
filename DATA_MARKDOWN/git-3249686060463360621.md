
# IU's Love Poem Becomes 5th Song Of 2019 To Score Perfect All-Kill

Published at: **2019-11-03T16:15:00+00:00**

Author: **BijayaMagar**

Original: [allkpop](https://www.allkpop.com/article/2019/11/ius-love-poem-becomes-5th-song-of-2019-to-score-perfect-all-kill)

On November 4 at approximately 0:30 AM KST,IU achieved a certified perfect all-kill on iChart with her newest single, “Love Poem”! 
A certified perfect all-kill is achieved after a song score first place on major daily and real-time charts including:Melon, Genie, Bugs, and Soribada, as well as Flo, and the weekly and realtime chart of Instiz’s iChart.“Love Poem” is the fifth song of the year to score a perfect all-kill, with the other songs being BTS’s “Boy With Luv” in April, BOL4’s “Workaholic” in September,”, AKMU’s “How Can I Love the Heartbreak, You’re the One I Love” in October and MC Mong’s “Fame” in October.
