
# Eugene Scalia

Published at: **2019-11-01T17:18:28+00:00**

Author: **BRIEF**

Original: [Courthouse News Service](https://www.courthousenews.com/eugene-scalia/)

WASHINGTON — Everglades Harvesting and Hauling et al. sued Labor Secretary Eugene Scalia in federal court, claiming he illegally reversed a policy on whether transportation of agricultural products to storage or processing constitutes agricultural labor for eligibility for the H-2A nonimmigrant visa program.
