
# DA: Police investigating suspicious death of Plymouth woman at Easton home

Published at: **2019-11-07T03:03:13+00:00**

Author: **EndPlay**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/da-police-investigating-suspicious-death-of-plymouth-woman-at-easton-home/1005924027?utm_source=homestream&utm_medium=site_navigation&utm_campaign=homestream_click)

EASTON, Mass. - Local and State Police are investigating the suspicious death of a 49-year-old Plymouth woman who was found dead in an Easton home Wednesday night, according to a release from the Bristol County District Attorney's Office. 
Easton PD found the victim dead at the scene from an apparent gunshot wound when they arrived at the home just before 6:30 p.m. on Wednesday night. Law enforcement responded to a home at 234 Washington Street in the town. 
Officials are investigating the incident as a suspicious death. The victim has not been identified yet as police are notifying the deceased's next of kin.
