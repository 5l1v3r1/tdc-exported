
# 10 Images Of Chiefs’ First Win Over Bucs In Four Years

Published at: **2019-11-03T09:31:00+00:00**

Author: **Soccer Laduma**

Original: [Soccer Laduma](https://www.soccerladuma.co.za/news/articles/local/categories/kaizer-chiefs/10-images-of-kaizer-chiefs-first-win-over-pirates-in-four-years/666609)

Kaizer Chiefs sent Orlando Pirates packing in the quarterfinals of the Telkom Knockout competition yesterday and Soccer Laduma takes a look at 10 standout moments of the game.
After leading 1-0 throughout the first half, the Buccaneers were reduced to 10 men in the second 45, when Mthokozisi Dube picked up a second yellow card and was given marching orders.

Consequently, Chiefs came back to make the score 2-1 in extra time, however, Gabadinho Mhango off the bench, clinched the equalizer to take their neighbours in to a penalty shoot-out.
However, Amakhosi once again triumphed through the penalty shoot-out, beating their neighbours for the first time since November 2015 and advancing through to the semi-finals of the competition.
