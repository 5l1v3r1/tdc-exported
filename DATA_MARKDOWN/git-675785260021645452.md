
# BREAKING Late Late Show legend Gay Byrne dies aged 85 after long illness

Published at: **2019-11-04T14:53:24+00:00**

Author: **Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/**

Original: [mirror](https://www.mirror.co.uk/3am/celebrity-news/breaking-late-late-show-legend-20809600?utm_source=twitter.com&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=sharebar)

Irish broadcasting legend Gay Byrne has died at the age of 85.
The former Late Late Show host had been ill for some time.
His sad passing was announced in a family statement.
It said: "It is with sadness that Kathleen, Crona and Suzy wish to announce that their beloved Gay has died peacefully at home today, surrounded by his family.
"We wish to thank everybody for their love and support during Gay's illness. Particularly the wonderful teams in the Mater Hospital, St Francis Hospice and the Irish Cancer Society."
Gay hosted the Late Late Show in Ireland for 37 years and had a career spanning six decades.
