
# Liverpool Equal All-Time EPL Record

Published at: **2019-11-04T17:00:00+00:00**

Author: **SL International**

Original: [Soccer Laduma](https://www.soccerladuma.co.za/news/articles/international/categories/english-premier-league/liverpool-equal-all-time-premier-league-record/666704)

Liverpool continued their charge towards the Premier League title on Saturday when they recording a win over Aston Villa, and in doing so, the Reds equalled an all-time league record in the process.

Jurgen Klopp's men were made to work hard for their victory over Villa, but came away with all three points courtesy of late goals from Andrew Robertson and Sadio Mane.
The win saw Liverpool maintain their six-point lead at the top of the table ahead of their clash against reigning champions Manchester City on Sunday, and also equal an all-time league record.

The Merseysiders have now earned 31 points from their opening 11 matches of the campaign, which is the joint-most by any club during the same period.
Chelsea were the first team to achieve the feat during the 2005/06 season, and were matched by City in 2011/12 and 2017/18, with both clubs going to win the title in each of those campaigns.
