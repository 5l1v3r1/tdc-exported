
# Scottish Junior Cup: Irvine Meadow to face Glenafton in tie of the fourth round

Published at: **2019-11-06T16:23:37+00:00**

Author: **Eric McGowan, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/eric-mcgowan/**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/ayrshire/scottish-junior-cup-irvine-meadow-20826226)

Irvine Meadow will host Ayrshire rivals Glenafton in the last 32 of the Scottish Junior Cup.
The West Premiership giants were paired together in this afternoon's draw in arguably the tie of the round.
Holders Auchinleck Talbot face Rossvale away while fellow big hitters Hurlford United are at home to Benburb.
 
The full draw is: Neilston v Lochee United; Gartcairn v Ellon Utd; Dundee North End v Kirkintiilloch Rob Roy; Blantyre Victoria v Darvel;  Hurlford v Benburb; Rossvale v Auchinleck Talbot; Bridge of Don Thistle v Fauldhouse Utd; Cambuslang Rangers v Broughty Athletic; Larkhall Thistle v Kello Rovers; Petershill v Rurtherglen; Aberdeen East End v Beith; Pollok v Troon; Kirrimuir v Tayport; Johnstone Burgh v St Roch's; Stoneywood Parkvale v Ashfield; Irvine Meadow v Glenafton.
Ties will be played on Saturday, November 23 at 1.45pm.
