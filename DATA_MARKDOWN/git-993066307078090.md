
# Everything you need to know about Apple TV+

Published at: **2019-11-01T13:32:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [The Irish News](http://www.irishnews.com/magazine/entertainment/2019/11/01/news/everything-you-need-to-know-about-apple-tv--1754320/)

Apple’s highly anticipated streaming service Apple TV+ has arrived, along with a range of programmes boasting big-name stars.
It will go head to head with current contenders Netflix and Amazon, as well as soon-to-be-unveiled Disney+ and BritBox.
If this is the start of the streaming wars, then Apple’s army is made up of top-tier Hollywood talent.
