
# Transport Corp Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 628.23 crore, up 1.28% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-06T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/transport-corp-standalone-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-628-23-crore-up-1-28-y-o-y-4608391.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Transport Corporation of India are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 628.23 crore in September 2019 up 1.28% from Rs. 620.26 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 33.27 crore in September 2019 up 28.6% from Rs. 25.87 crore in September 2018.

        Close
      
EBITDA stands at Rs. 64.92 crore in September 2019 up 6.04% from Rs. 61.22 crore in September 2018.
Transport Corp EPS has increased to Rs. 4.33 in September 2019 from Rs. 3.37 in September 2018.
Transport Corp shares closed at 284.65 on November 05, 2019 (NSE) and has given -0.54% returns over the last 6 months and 5.64% over the last 12 months.
