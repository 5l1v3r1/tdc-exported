
# Kartik Aaryan's 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' has a reprised version of 'Akhiyon Se Goli Maare'; here's what Raveena Tandon has to say about it

Published at: **2019-11-06T09:36:45+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/kartik-aaryans-pati-patni-aur-woh-has-a-reprised-version-of-akhiyon-se-goli-maare-heres-what-raveena-tandon-has-to-say-about-it/articleshow/71937529.cms)

The trailer of Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday's upcoming film 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' which was released by the makers of the film on Monday this week saw a great response from the audience as well as other B-town celebs.
The trio has managed to weave their magic in the trailer and the film which is a remake of 1978 Hindi film with the same title which featured Sanjeev Kumar, Vidya Sinha and Ranjeeta Kaur looks to be quite promising.
Helmed by Mudassar Aziz, the trailer also had a reprised version of Govinda and Raveena Tandon popular song 'Akhiyon Se Goli Maare' playing in the background.
