
# Victim identified in fatal Thursday shooting in Roslindale

Published at: **2019-11-03T04:04:17+00:00**

Author: **EndPlay**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/victim-identified-in-fatal-thursday-shooting-in-roslindale/1004381419)

ROSLINDALE, Mass. - Boston Police have identified the victim of a fatal Thursday shooting in Roslindale, according to an official release from the department. 
Pasqual Casiano, 44, of Jamaica Plain was shot to death on Thursday afternoon in the area of 581 American Legion Highway, police say.
A second male victim was hospitalized after the shooting as well; police say that person sustained non-life-threatening injuries. 
Casiano died from injuries sustained after being transported to a local hospital when police arrived at the scene of the alleged shooting. The incident occurred around 2 p.m. on Thursday afternoon.
Anyone with information on the shooting is asked to call Boston Police detectives at 617-343-4470.
