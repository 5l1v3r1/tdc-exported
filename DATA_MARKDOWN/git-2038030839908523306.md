
# Chrissy Teigen's parents to divorce

Published at: **2019-11-06T18:00:12+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Channel24](https://www.channel24.co.za/Gossip/News/chrissy-teigens-parents-to-divorce-20191106)

Cape Town - The Lip Sync Battle star's dad, Ron Teigen, has filed documents to dissolve his marriage to wife Vilailuck, People magazine reports.
The reason behind the split is unknown.
Chrissy previously revealed that while her mom lives with her, husband John Legend and their children Luna (3), and Miles (17 months), her dad lives around 10 minutes away but she insisted it was an arrangement that "worked" for them.
