
# France's EDF buys electric vehicle charging point firm Pivot Power

Published at: **2019-11-04T08:23:33+00:00**

Author: **Reuters**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/frances-edf-buys-electric-vehicle-charging-point-firm-pivot-power)

(Nov 4): French state-controlled energy group EDF has agreed to buy Pivot Power, a British start-up company that specialises in battery storage and electric vehicle charging points.
EDF, which did not disclose the financial terms of the acquisition, said the deal would allow it to become a leader in the fast-growing field of battery storage.
