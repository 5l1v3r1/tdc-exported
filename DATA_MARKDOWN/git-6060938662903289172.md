
# Awwwdorable! Fans gift Kareena Kapoor Khan a painting of Taimur Ali Khan in Australia

Published at: **2019-11-03T09:24:21+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/awwwdorable-fans-gift-kareena-kapoor-khan-a-painting-of-taimur-ali-khan-in-australia/articleshow/71876352.cms)

Taimur Ali Khan has fans all over the world and here’s proof of his popularity in Australia. Taimur’s mom, the ever so glamorous Kareena Kapoor Khan attended an event in Sydney yesterday and fans gifted her an adorable painting of her toddler. Fans thronged the actress on the stage where she was gifted the painting. Bebo seemed impressed by the portrait and happily posed with the group.
