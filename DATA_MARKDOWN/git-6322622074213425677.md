
# Tesla has 42,000 employees globally, 3,200 in China, chairwoman says

Published at: **2019-11-05T09:13:24+00:00**

Author: **Reuters**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/tesla-has-42000-employees-globally-3200-china-chairwoman-says)

SHANGHAI (Nov 5): U.S. electric vehicle maker Tesla Inc has 42,000 employees globally and 3,200 staff in China, its chairwoman Robyn Denholm said on Tuesday.
Denholm was speaking on the sidelines of a week-long import expo in Shanghai, where Tesla has started trial production at its $2 billion factory as it races to reach an ambitious target of an annualized production rate of 500,000 vehicles by end-2019.
