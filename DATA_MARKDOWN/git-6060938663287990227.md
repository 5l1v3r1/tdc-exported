
# Farhan Akhtar gives a glimpse of onlookers from the sets of 'Toofan'

Published at: **2019-11-06T12:34:08+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/farhan-akhtar-gives-a-glimpse-of-onlookers-from-the-sets-of-toofan/articleshow/71940597.cms)

Farhan Akhtar has sweating it out in the ring since the last few months as he has been learning the nuances of boxing for his upcoming film 'Toofan'. Even after suffering an injuring his hand during the training, Farhan continued to commence with the shoot of the film.
Taking to Instagram, Farhan shared a glimpse of onlookers from the sets of the film. The cast and crew are shooting in the Dongri area of Mumbai and this picture of people looking out of the window showcases the love that the people have for cinema in our country.
While Farhan's recent outing at the box office 'The Sky is Pink' which even featured Priyanka Chopra, failed to impress the audience, the versatile actor is leaving no stone unturned with 'Toofan'.
The film which is being helmed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra is a romantic flick which will revolve around the life of a boxer. The first look poster has been well received by all and has had fans eagerly awaiting its release on 2nd October 2020.
