
# Jermaine Whitehead cut by Cleveland Browns after posting violent threats on Twitter

Published at: **2019-11-04T15:10:42+00:00**

Author: **CBS/AP**

Original: [CBS News](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/jermaine-whitehead-twitter-cleveland-browns-cut-player-today-disturbing-social-media-rant-2019-11-04/)

Browns safety Jermaine Whitehead has been released following his disturbing social media rant after a loss in Denver. The Browns announced the move Monday, hours after rebuking Whitehead for "totally unacceptable and highly inappropriate behavior" following a 24-19 loss to the Broncos on Sunday.
