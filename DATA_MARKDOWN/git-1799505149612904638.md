
# Inslee Recall Petition

Published at: **2019-11-05T08:24:41+00:00**

Author: **BRIEF**

Original: [Courthouse News Service](https://www.courthousenews.com/inslee-recall-petition/)

OLYMPIA, Wash. – The Supreme Court of Washington ruled that a recall petition against Governor Jay Inslee was “legally and factually insufficient to submit to voters.” A man claimed Inslee was away from Washington too frequently and failed to declare homelessness a statewide emergency.
