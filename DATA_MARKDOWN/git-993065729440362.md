
# Irish man arrested after 15 people found in back of lorry

Published at: **2019-11-07T11:00:00+00:00**

Author: **Rod Minchin, Press Association**

Original: [The Irish News](http://www.irishnews.com/news/republicofirelandnews/2019/11/07/news/irish-man-arrested-after-15-people-found-in-back-of-lorry-1759311/)

An Irish man in his 50s has been arrested after 15 people were found in the back of a lorry in Wiltshire, England.
Police said they were received a call shortly before 8.30pm last night by a member of the public reporting "suspicious activity" involving the vehicle.
The 15 people who were in the back of the vehicle have been arrested on suspicion of immigration offences.
The driver has been arrested on suspicion of assisting with illegal entry in the country.
Wiltshire Police closed the A350 at the Kington Langley crossroads near Chippenham after arriving at the scene.
