
# Central Bank Standalone September 2019 Net Interest Income (NII) at Rs 1,890.96 crore, up 12.86% Y-o-Y

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/central-bank-standalone-september-2019-net-interest-income-nii-at-rs-1890-96-crore-up-12-86-y-o-y-4599991.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Central Bank of India are:
      
Net Interest Income (NII) at Rs 1,890.96 crore in September 2019 up 12.86% from Rs. 1675.5 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 134.07 crore in September 2019 up 114.52% from Rs. 923.60 crore in September 2018.
