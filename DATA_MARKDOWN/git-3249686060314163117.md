
# MFBTY drop trippy animated MV teaser for 'Dream Catcher'

Published at: **2019-11-07T09:35:00+00:00**

Author: **germainej**

Original: [allkpop](https://www.allkpop.com/video/2019/11/mfbty-drop-trippy-animated-mv-teaser-for-dream-catcher)

MFBTY have dropped a trippy animated MV teaser for their upcoming release "Dream Catcher".The MV teaser reveals a trippy, hip hop theme with animated versions of Tiger JK, Yoon Mi Rae, and Bizzy as well as an interesting beat featuring a lot of brass. MFBTY's "Dream Catcher" drops on November 10 KST.
