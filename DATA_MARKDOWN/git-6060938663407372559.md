
# Photos: Shraddha Kapoor and Sara Ali Khan flash their sweet smiles as they pose for pictures together at 'Bala' screening

Published at: **2019-11-06T19:06:58+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/photos-shraddha-kapoor-and-sara-ali-khan-flash-their-sweet-smiles-as-they-pose-for-pictures-together-at-bala-screening/articleshow/71946299.cms)

Ayushmann Khurrana, Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar's 'Bala' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. Ahead of the release, the makers of the film hosted a screening for the friends from the fraternity.
Shraddha Kapoor and Sara Ali Khan were also among the celebs who attended the screening. For the occasion, Shraddha opted for a turquoise green floral print dress while Sara was in a light blue outfit. The screening was also attended by Varun Dhawan, Ileana D'Cruz, Janhvi Kapoor, Boney Kapoor and other celebs.
On the work front, Shraddha will be next seen in Remo D'Souza's 'Street Dancer 3D' co-starring Varun Dhawan. On the other hand, Sara will feature in Imtiaz Ali's next which also stars Kartik Aaryan.
Meanwhile, 'Bala' helmed by Amar Kaushik will hit the theatres on November 08.
