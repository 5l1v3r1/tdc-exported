
# Seacera says Fintec is now a substantial shareholder

Published at: **2019-11-06T06:15:47+00:00**

Author: **Chong Jin Hun**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/seacera-says-fintec-now-substantial-shareholder)

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 6): Tile manufacturer Seacera Group Bhd said today Fintec Global Bhd has become a substantial shareholder in Seacera after Fintec's wholly-owned subsidiary Asiabio Capital Sdn Bhd acquired 38.8 million shares or an 8.078% stake in Seacera.
In statements to Bursa Malaysia today, Seacera said Asiabio had acquired the Seacera shares on the open market on Monday (Nov 4).
Fintec's website indicates that the group provides funding for start-up companies in sectors including renewable energy, oil and gas besides multimedia solutions.
Yesterday, Fintec said in its Bursa filing that Asiabio, which is involved in the trading of marketable securities, had acquired the 38.8 million Seacera shares for RM18.59 million.
At Bursa's afternoon break today, Seacera's share price settled down 0.5 sen or 1.49% at 33 sen. The stock saw 3.9 million shares traded.
