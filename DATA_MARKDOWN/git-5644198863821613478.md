
# California Governor to convene meet of PG&E shareholders, execs next week

Published at: **2019-11-02T05:45:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [CNA](https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/business/california-governor-to-convene-meet-of-pg-e-shareholders--execs-next-week-12056704?utm_source=dlvr.it&utm_medium=twitter)

California Governor Gavin Newsom said he will convene a meeting of PG&E shareholders and executives in Sacramento next week to speed up the resolution of the bankruptcy case that creates a new entity.
REUTERS: California Governor Gavin Newsom said he will convene a meeting of PG&E shareholders and executives in Sacramento next week to speed up the resolution of the bankruptcy case that creates a new entity.
The new company can only happen "if we first get out of bankruptcy court," Newsom said in a statement on Friday, without elaborating his plans.
The San Francisco-based power producer in January filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection anticipating its liabilities from massive wildfires in 2017 and 2018 blamed on its equipment could top US$30 billion.
