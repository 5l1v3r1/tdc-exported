
# 10-year-old boy shot in back of head, critically injured; gunman at-large

Published at: **2019-11-07T11:41:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [ABC News](https://abcnews.go.com/US/10-year-philadelphia-boy-shot-back-head-critical/story?id=66801960)

A 10-year-old boy was shot in the back of the head and critically injured in broad daylight a Philadelphia street, and now authorities say they're searching for the unknown gunman.
The shooting happened Wednesday just after 3 p.m. in the city's Frankford section, according to the Philadelphia Police Department, and no arrests have been made.
The little boy, identified by family as Semaj O'Branty, was walking home from school at the time, according to ABC Philadelphia station WPVI. The shooting was captured on surveillance video.
