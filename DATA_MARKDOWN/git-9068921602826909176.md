
# Apple Shares Trailer for Upcoming Apple TV+ Movie 'Hala'

Published at: **2019-11-05T12:18:35+00:00**

Author: **Juli Clover**

Original: [MacRumors](https://www.macrumors.com/2019/11/05/apple-shares-hala-movie-trailer/)

Apple today shared the official trailer for "Hala," an upcoming movie that is set to debut on Apple TV+ on December 6. "Hala" is a coming of age story of 17-year-old Hala, who is played by Geraldine Viswanathan. Anna Chlumsky, Jack Kilmer, Gabriel Luna, Azad Khan, and Purbi Joshi also star in the movie.
