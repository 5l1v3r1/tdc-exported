
# Suspect sought in Punta Gorda vehicle burglaries

Published at: **2019-11-06T11:12:07+00:00**

Author: **WFTX Digital Team**

Original: [Fox 4](https://www.fox4now.com/news/local-news/suspect-sought-in-punta-gorda-vehicle-burglaries)

PUNTA GORDA, Fla. -- Police have released a surveillance image of a suspect in several vehicle burglaries in Punta Gorda.
According to Punta Gorda Police, somebody illegally entered several vehicles on West Henry Street and stole several items.
It happened in the early morning hours of October 29th.
If you have any information on this incident, please contact Southwest Florida Crime Stoppers at 1-800-780-TIPS, by visiting www.southwestfloridacrimestoppers.com , or through the P3 Tips mobile app.
You could be eligible for a cash reward for information leading to an arrest.
