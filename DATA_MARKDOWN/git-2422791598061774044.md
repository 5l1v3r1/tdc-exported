
# Ontario trucker fined for watching TV while driving

Published at: **2019-11-07T14:20:00+00:00**

Author: **Meredith MacLeod, CTVNews.ca Writer**

Original: [www.ctvnews.ca](https://www.ctvnews.ca/canada/ontario-trucker-fined-for-watching-tv-while-driving-1.4674144)

TORONTO -- Police in Ontario have charged a transport truck driver after other motorists on Highway 401 witnessed a swerving truck with a screen playing in its cab late Tuesday night.
Ontario Provincial Police pulled over the Toronto driver, who was hauling two sea containers, near Napanee, Ont. at about 11:30 p.m. They found a screen mounted to the dashboard that was playing a TV show.
The 38-year-old was given a $615 fine for driving with a display screen visible to the driver.
