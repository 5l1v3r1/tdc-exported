
# Video: UFC 244 Post-Fight Press Conference Live Stream For Jorge Masvidal Vs. Nate Diaz Card

Published at: **2019-11-02T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: **Trent Reinsmith**

Original: [Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/sites/trentreinsmith/2019/11/02/video-ufc-244-post-fight-press-conference-live-stream-for-jorge-masvidal-vs-nate-diaz-card/)

The UFC is in New York City, for tonight’s UFC 244 pay-per-view card. In the headlining bout, Jorge Masvidal and Nate Diaz meet in a welterweight bout that will earn the victor the UFC BMF title, a belt that was spoken into existence after Nate Diaz defeated Anthony Pettis at UFC 241.In the co-main event, former UFC interim middleweight title challenger Kelvin Gastelum welcomes ex-welterweight title challenger Darren Till to the 185-pound division.
Also on the pay-per-view card, ex-heavyweight title challenger looks to end a two-fight losing skid against Blagoy Ivanov. 
Following the main event, the UFC will hold the post-fight press conference for the fight card. The competitors from the BMF title fight, as well as other main card fighters, are expected to spend some time with the media after the headlining contest ends.
UFC 244 takes place Saturday, November 2 at Madison Square Garden in New York City. The main card airs on ESPN+ pay-per-view following prelims on ESPN2 and UFC Fight Pass/ESPN+.
The post-fight press conference is expected to begin around 1:15 a.m. ET.
