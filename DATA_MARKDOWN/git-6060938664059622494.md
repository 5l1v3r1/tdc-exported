
# ‘Panipat’ trailer: Akshay Kumar, Vicky Kaushal and other B-town stars laud Arjun Kapoor and Sanjay Dutt’s epic war drama

Published at: **2019-11-05T08:54:50+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/panipat-trailer-akshay-kumar-vicky-kaushal-and-other-b-town-stars-laud-arjun-kapoor-and-sanjay-dutts-epic-war-drama/articleshow/71920168.cms)

Director Ashutosh Gowarikar dropped the trailer of his magnum opus 'Panipat' featuring Arjun Kapoor, Sanjay Dutt and Kriti Sanon in lead roles. The trailer that gives us glimpses of all that lead up to the third battle of Panipat that saw the Maratha army lead by Sadashiv Rao Bhau wage war against the invading Afghan army lead by Ahmad Shah Abdali, left many spellbound.
Packed with gory battle scenes, the clinching of swords, epic dialogues, gripping soundtrack and even some tender moments between Arjun and Kriti’s characters, the trailer promises an all-round entertainer.
As soon as the trailer launched online, stars like Akshay Kumar, Vicky Kaushal, Karan Johar, Farah Khan and others took to social media to laud the team for their efforts and the impressive trailer.
