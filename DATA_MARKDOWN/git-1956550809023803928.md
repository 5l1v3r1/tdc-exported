
# Dubai’s BOCA to host special zero-waste dinner

Published at: **2019-11-07T08:26:00+00:00**

Author: **Reema Rahman**

Original: [Time Out Dubai](https://www.timeoutdubai.com/restaurants/422189-dubais-boca-to-host-special-zero-waste-dinner)

DIFC’s BOCA, which is already well-known for sourcing local and sustainable ingredients, will be hosting environmental activist and film producer Jo Ruxton for a special zero-waste dinner on Wednesday November 27.The evening kicks off at 7pm with a welcome reception and a 20-minute version of Jo Ruxton’s award-winning documentary film ‘A Plastic Ocean’ – complete with sustainable movie snacks and drinks, of course.The short movie screening will be followed by a zero waste dinner at 8pm.The forward-thinking kitchen will be drawing attention to lesser known local produce and maximising the usage of food, and paired with environmentally-conscious juices for Dhs195 or grape for Dhs295.Looking for more environmentally-friendly places to try?
