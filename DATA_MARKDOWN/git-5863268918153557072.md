
# PAF demonstrates its power at Sonmiani Firing Range

Published at: **2019-11-06T12:05:00+00:00**

Author: **https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews**

Original: [Dunya News](https://dunyanews.tv/en/Pakistan/517454-PAF-demonstrates-its-power-at-Sonmiani-Firing-Range)

KARACHI (Dunya News) – The Pakistan Air Force (PAF) on Wednesday flexed its muscles at Sonmiani Firing Range.
President Arif Alvi, Air Chief Marshall Mujahid Anwar Khan, Chairman Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee General Zubair Mehmood Hayart among others observed the PAF’s firepower demonstration.
TV footage available with the media showed JF-17 Thunder Aircraft, helicopter gunships and Commandos of Special Services Wing displayed their professional skills.
Meanwhile, other fighter aircrafts are flying at lightning speed and dropping bombs on the designated targets.
Earlier, the air force produced a promo regarding PAF’s firepower demonstration.
