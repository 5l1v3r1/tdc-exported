
# Genting Singapore posts 24% drop in 3Q earnings to S$159 mil on lower gaming revenue

Published at: **2019-11-07T11:20:13+00:00**

Author: **Uma Devi**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/genting-singapore-posts-24-drop-3q-earnings-s159-mil-lower-gaming-revenue)

SINGAPORE (Nov 11): Genting Singapore (GENS) reported earnings of S$158.9 million for 3QFY19 ended September, some 24% lower than S$210.4 million a year ago.
Revenue for the quarter fell 7% to S$596.1 million from S$639.1 million in 3QFY18, which the group attributed mainly to “a confluence of headwinds”.
