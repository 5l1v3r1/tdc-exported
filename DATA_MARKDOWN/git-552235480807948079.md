
# O2 Priority presale tickets to see Westlife in Falkirk and Inverness are on sale today

Published at: **2019-11-06T08:11:19+00:00**

Author: **Linda Howard**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/lifestyle/things-to-do/o2-priority-presale-tickets-see-20821793)

Presales of tickets to see Westlife perform in Falkirk and Inverness next year as part of their 'Stadiums in the Summer' tour started on Tuesday.
Fans who pre-ordered the Irish band's new album ‘Spectrum’ on any format gained unique access to the presale through Ticketmaster on Tuesday.
But for those that missed out, 9am on Wednesday sees the start of the O2 Priority presale .
If you're on the O2 mobile network, getting presale tickets is easy - follow our simple steps below and you'll be able to buy your Westlife tickets as soon as they dorp.

        How to set up O2 Priority
      
You can order presale tickets on the app or on the O2 Priority website here .
And if that seems like too much hassle for a Wednesday morning - just find a friend that already has O2 Priority access and ask them order for you!
 
The ‘ Stadiums in the Summer ’ announcement follows the phenomenal success of Westlife's remarkable comeback and comes as the band prepares to release their brand new album ‘Spectrum’ on November 15.
Nicky Byrne, Shane Filan, Mark Feehily and Kian Egan will perform at Falkirk Stadium , Grangemouth and Tulloch Caledonian Stadium , Inverness next June, with tickets going on general sale at 9am on Friday, November 8.
