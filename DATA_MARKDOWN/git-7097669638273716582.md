
# Bill O’Reilly: 'If Joe Biden is elected president ... he has to be impeached'

Published at: **2019-11-01T15:19:45+00:00**

Author: **Yahoo News Video**

Original: [Yahoo](https://news.yahoo.com/bill-o-reilly-joe-biden-151945880.html)

Disgraced former Fox News host Bill O’Reilly shared his dream of what should happen if Joe Biden were to win the Democratic nomination and then defeat President Trump in 2020.
“If Joe Biden is elected president, the day after he’s sworn in, he has to be impeached," said O'Reilly.
