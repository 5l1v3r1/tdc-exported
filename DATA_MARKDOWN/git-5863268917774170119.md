
# Bilawal Bhutto expresses concerns over Zardari's health condition

Published at: **2019-11-05T13:23:00+00:00**

Author: **https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews**

Original: [Dunya News](https://dunyanews.tv/en/Pakistan/517311-Bilawal-Bhutto-concerns-Zardari-health-condition-)

KARACHI (Dunya News) – Pakistan Peoples’ Party (PPP) Chairman Bilawal Bhutto on Tuesday has expressed his concerns over the health of his father and former president Asif Ali Zardari. In a post on social-networking website Twitter, the PPP leader said, “President Zardari has still not been given access to specialist doctors and personal physician. Our family is increasingly concerned about his health.”If anything happens to our father this government will be held responsible, he warned.
