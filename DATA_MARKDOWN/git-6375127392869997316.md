
# 9 NPA rebs, 46 others arrested

Published at: **2019-11-02T10:50:46+00:00**

Author: **Zaida Delos Reyes**

Original: [Journal Online](https://journal.com.ph/news/metro/9-npa-rebs-46-others-arrested)

This was confirmed yesterday by  Captain Cenon C. Pancito, spokesman of Philippine Army’s 3rd Infantry Division ( ID).
Pancito identified those considered  high value individuals of the NPA as  John Milton Lozande, Noli Lazeran Rosales, Proceso Quiatchon, Albert Delacerna, Mary Ann Krueger alias Mary Anne Dela Concepcion,  Romulo Buto-on, Amatlin Chin-Chin  Bito-on, Danilo Tabura and Roberto Lachica.
Pancito said the troops conducted simultaneous implementation of search warrants for violation of  RA 10591 or  Comprehensive Forearms and Ammunition  Regulation Law issued by Quezon City Regional Trial Court (RTC)  in Barangays  33, Bata and Taculing all in Bacolod City  at around 5:45 p.m. on October 31,2019.
The operation resulted in the recovery of 32 assorted firearms, 3 fragmentation grenade, 2 ammunition of 40MM Grenade Launcher and assorted live ammunition.
Pancito said the operation also resulted in the accidental arrest of 40 identified members of Komiteng Rehiyon-Negros, Cebu, Bohol and Siquijor (KR-NCBS) and 15 newly recruited individuals, 14 of them minors coming from different areas of Negros Occidental who are undergoing combat-related training and indoctrination .
The minors were turned over to the custody of the City Social Welfare Development Office (CSDWO).
