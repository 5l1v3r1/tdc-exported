Published at: **2019-11-07T16:35:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Allhockey.ru](https://allhockey.ru/news/show/369293-Sbornaya_Belarusi_odolela_Franciju_v_1_m_matche_posle_vozvrascheniya_Zaharova)
