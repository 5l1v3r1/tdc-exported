
# Police rush to scene after man, 40, dies on Glasgow Street

Published at: **2019-11-06T14:22:58+00:00**

Author: **Lynn Love**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/news/scottish-news/police-rush-scene-after-man-20824817)

A 40-year-old man was found dead on a Glasgow street last night.
Police were called to Parkneuk Road, Mansewood at around 9.50pm following the sudden death of the victim.
There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.
