
# Romania government nominates two candidates for EU commissioner

Published at: **2019-11-06T12:25:18+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/article/us-eu-commission-romania-idUSKBN1XG1SM)

BUCHAREST (Reuters) - Romania’s centrist government on Wednesday nominated two candidates for EU Commisioner, European lawmakers Adina Valean and Siegfried Muresan, Prime Minister Ludovic Orban said.
“After consultations with Romania’s president, we decided to nominate two candidates,” Orban told reporters.
