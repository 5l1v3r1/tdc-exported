
# Man jailed after brandishing broken glass at police at hospital

Published at: **2019-11-05T16:29:15+00:00**

Author: **Tara Fitzpatrick**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/ayrshire/man-jailed-after-brandishing-broken-20817869)

A man who brandished a broken glass at police at Crosshouse Hospital has been jailed.
Samuel McGovern challenged the police officers to a fight and waved a piece of glass around before being arrested.
McGovern, whose address was given as Kilmarnock Prison, pleaded guilty to behaving in a threatening and abusive manner and obstructing police officers by throwing himself on the ground.
The offences took place at Crosshouse Hospital and Vernon Street in Saltcoats, North Ayrshire on June 16.
The 28-year-old was jailed for 10 months.
