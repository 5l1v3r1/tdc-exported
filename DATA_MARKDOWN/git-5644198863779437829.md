
# Second man pleads guilty to attempted robbery of Arsenal stars

Published at: **2019-11-06T23:05:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [CNA](https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/world/jordan-northover-arsenal-robbery-mesut-ozil-sead-kolasinac-12070100)

LONDON: A second man pleaded guilty on Wednesday (Nov 6) to trying to rob Arsenal stars Mesut Ozil and Sead Kolasinac of their watches in a moped ambush.
Jordan Northover, 26, admitted trying to steal the expensive items from the Premier League footballers in northwest London on Jul 25, a court heard.
Ashley Smith has already pleaded guilty to his role in the attempted raid involving close friends Ozil, 31, and Kolasinac, 26.
