
# Liverpool vs Genk half-time player ratings: Georginio Wijnaldum goal cancelled out by Mbwana Samatta

Published at: **2019-11-05T20:59:36+00:00**

Author: **Mark Critchley**

Original: [The Independent](https://www.independent.co.uk/sport/football/european/liverpool-vs-genk-player-ratings-halftime-champions-league-georginio-wijnaldum-goal-a9186781.html?utm_medium=Social&utm_source=Twitter)

Liverpool’s first-half lead was cancelled out just before half-time in their Champions League clash with Genk.
The Reds started well and took the lead through Georginio Wijnaldum.
The Netherlands international headed in James Milner’s fine cross to give his side the lead.
Liverpool looked in complete control, only for Genk forward Mbwana Samatta to level the score with a powerful header at the near post.
