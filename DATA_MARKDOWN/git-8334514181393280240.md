
# Hong Kong police fire water cannon at Guy Fawkes protesters

Published at: **2019-11-05T13:11:21+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/article/us-hongkong-protests-water-idUSKBN1XF1QL)

HONG KONG (Reuters) - Hong Kong police fired water cannon at protesters wearing Guy Fawkes masks and crowding the tourist streets of Tsim Sha Tsui where at least one restaurant was vandalized.
