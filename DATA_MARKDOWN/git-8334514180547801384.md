
# Baghdad blast caused by sound bomb, no casualties: police sources

Published at: **2019-11-05T18:50:41+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/article/us-iraq-security-blast-idUSKBN1XF2CU)

BAGHDAD (Reuters) - A loud explosion heard in the Iraqi capital Baghdad was caused by a sound bomb going off near a bridge where anti-government protesters have been amassing this week, police sources said on Tuesday.
The explosion caused no casualties, the sources said.
