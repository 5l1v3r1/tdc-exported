
# Kalyani Invest Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 15.94 crore, down 12.91% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-04T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/kalyani-invest-standalone-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-15-94-crore-down-12-91-y-o-y-4601261.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Kalyani Investment Company are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 15.94 crore in September 2019 down 12.91% from Rs. 18.31 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 16.14 crore in September 2019 down 9.32% from Rs. 17.80 crore in September 2018.

        Close
      
EBITDA stands at Rs. 15.53 crore in September 2019 down 13.14% from Rs. 17.88 crore in September 2018.
Kalyani Invest EPS has decreased to Rs. 36.98 in September 2019 from Rs. 40.78 in September 2018.
Kalyani Invest shares closed at 1,730.00 on November 01, 2019 (NSE) and has given -3.47% returns over the last 6 months and -13.02% over the last 12 months.
