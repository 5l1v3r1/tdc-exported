
# Passengers and crew safely off plane at Schiphol: military police

Published at: **2019-11-06T20:00:34+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/article/us-netherlands-schiphol-airplane-idUSKBN1XG2RW)

AMSTERDAM (Reuters) - The passengers and crew of an airplane at Amsterdam Schiphol airport were safely evacuated on Wednesday after authorities said they were investigating “a suspicious situation.”
The military police said: “Passengers and crew are safely off the plane. An investigation is ongoing.”
No additional details were provided.
