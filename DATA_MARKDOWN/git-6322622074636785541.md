
# PetGas PDT, IDSS suspended until Monday

Published at: **2019-11-01T07:51:26+00:00**

Author: **Arjuna Chandran Shankar**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/petgas-pdt-idss-suspended-until-monday)

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 1): Proprietary day trading (PDT) and intra-day short selling (IDSS) of Petronas Gas Bhd (PetGas) have been suspended after its share price fell more than 15%.
The regulator Bursa Malaysia said PDT and IDSS for the counter will only resume next Monday (Nov 4) at 8:30am.
"Please be informed that short selling under PDT and IDSS for stock PetGas (6033) has been suspended for the rest of the day as the Last Done Price of the Approved Securities dropped more than 15% from the Reference Price," it said in a bourse filing.
As at 3:12pm, shares in PetGas were 2.4% or 40 sen lower at RM16.50, giving it a market capitalisation of RM32.65 billion. The counter saw 152,200 shares traded.
