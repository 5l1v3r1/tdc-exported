
# One dead from a plane crash at Stuart Air Show

Published at: **2019-11-01T12:52:25+00:00**

Author: **WPTV Webteam**

Original: [Fox 4](https://www.fox4now.com/news/state/one-dead-from-a-plane-crash-at-stuart-air-show)

The Martin County Sheriff's Office says one person died when a plane crashed at the Stuart Air Show on Friday afternoon.
The Federal Aviation Administration and the National Transportation Safety Board said they're investigating the crash of a Grumman OV-1 Mohawk.
The FAA said the Mohawk, with only the pilot aboard, crashed on the approach end of Runway 1 at Witham Field around 1:15 p.m.
