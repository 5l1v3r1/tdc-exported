
# Expleo Solution Consolidated September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 65.67 crore, down 13.34% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-01T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/expleo-solution-consolidated-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-65-67-crore-down-13-34-y-o-y-4595231.html)


        Reported Consolidated quarterly numbers for Expleo Solutions are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 65.67 crore in September 2019 down 13.34% from Rs. 75.78 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 8.95 crore in September 2019 down 34.42% from Rs. 13.65 crore in September 2018.

        Close
      
EBITDA stands at Rs. 14.48 crore in September 2019 down 34.6% from Rs. 22.14 crore in September 2018.
Expleo Solution EPS has decreased to Rs. 8.55 in September 2019 from Rs. 12.74 in September 2018.
Expleo Solution shares closed at 291.45 on October 31, 2019 (NSE) and has given -38.93% returns over the last 6 months and -33.73% over the last 12 months.
