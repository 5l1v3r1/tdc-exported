
# Azadi March: Protester dies of heart attack

Published at: **2019-11-05T10:40:00+00:00**

Author: **https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews**

Original: [Dunya News](https://dunyanews.tv/en/Pakistan/517279-Azadi-March-Protester-dies-of-heart-attack)

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – A protester, who had been participating in opposition’s Azadi March in Islamabad, died on Tuesday morning of a heart attack.
Reportedly, 55-year-old Abdul Kareem Jan, hailing from Larkana, was rushed to Pims Hospital where doctors declared him dead on arrival.
