
# Elections

Published at: **2019-11-04T15:47:46+00:00**

Original: [Courthouse News Service](https://www.courthousenews.com/elections-107/)

PHOENIX — The Democratic National Committee sued the Arizona secretary of state in federal court for using a method of ballot-creation that will list all Republican candidates first in all statewide races in 11 of the state’s 15 counties; the DNC filed a similar lawsuit against Texas, in Austin Federal Court; and against Georgia, in Atlanta.
