
# Wawona recalls frozen raspberries because of possible hepatitis A contamination

Published at: **2019-11-03T17:13:49+00:00**

Author: **Bob D&#39;Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/trending-now/wawona-recalls-frozen-raspberries-because-of-possible-hepatitis-a-contamination/1004652615?utm_source=homestream&utm_medium=site_navigation&utm_campaign=homestream_click)

Wawona Frozen Foods is voluntarily recalling packages of frozen raspberries and frozen berry mixes containing raspberries after it was discovered some of them were potentially contaminated with the hepatitis A virus, according to a news release from the Food and Drug Administration.
The raspberries, sold at Aldi Grocery Stores and Raley's Family of Fine Stores, were recalled Thursday, according to Friday's news release by the FDA.
Products affected include Aldi's frozen "Season’s Choice Raspberries" and "Season’s Choice Berry Medley" products, and "Raley’s Fresh Frozen Red Raspberries." 
No illnesses associated with the recall have been reported. the FDA said.
Hepatitis A is a contagious virus that can cause liver disease, according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention website.
