Published at: **2019-11-07T23:14:31+00:00**

This evening, Apple has announced its latest Apple Pay promotion. Over the next three days, you can score $5 off your Uber Eats order when you check out with Apple Pay.
The promotion runs from November 8 through November 10. During this period, you can get $5 off your order of $10 or more when you use Apple Pay to checkout and enter code APPLEPAY5 at checkout.
