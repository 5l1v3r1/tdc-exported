
# Fake picture of Rabi Pirzada's suicide attempt doing rounds on social media

Published at: **2019-11-04T00:12:00+00:00**

Author: **https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews**

Original: [Dunya News](https://dunyanews.tv/en/FakeNews/517068-Fake-picture-of-Rabi-Pirzada%E2%80%99s-suicide-attempt-doing-rounds-)

LAHORE (Web Desk) - Rabi Pirzada’s suicide rumors after leaked nude photos and Videos have vent viral on social media.
A picture is doing rounds on social media showing that the star singer Rabi Pirzada has done suicide attempt after her immoral videos went viral on social media yesterday and became a top trend on Twitter, after which she was heavily criticized on social media.
However Humayun Pirzada, father of singer Rabbi Pirzada, denied the news of his daughter s suicide.
The picture does not belong to Rabi Pirzada as is mentioned in the said post.
Rabbi Pirzada has filed a complaint into the Federal Investigation Agency s cybercrime wing Lahore against her leaked videos.
FIA Cyber Crime Wing investigated the case after the singer s nude photos and videos went viral on social media.
