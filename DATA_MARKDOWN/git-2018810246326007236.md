
# Chinese Player Xiaomeng VKLiooon Li Has Won The Hearthstone Grand Finals At Blizzcon 2019.

Published at: **2019-11-03T10:30:00+00:00**

Author: **Heather Alexandra**

Original: [Kotaku Australia](https://www.kotaku.com.au/2019/11/chinese-player-xiaomeng-vkliooon-li-has-won-the-hearthstone-grand-finals-at-blizzcon-2019/)

Chinese player Xiaomeng “VKLiooon” Li has won the Hearthstone Grand Finals at Blizzcon 2019. She is the first female grand finals winner ever.
