
# Merry Coffee: Starbucks holiday cups, drinks now available

Published at: **2019-11-07T15:08:39+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [ABC7 Los Angeles](https://abc7.com/food/merry-coffee-starbucks-holiday-cups-drinks-now-available/5675898/)
