
# Titan Company Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 4,435.00 crore, up 0.64% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-06T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/titan-company-standalone-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-4435-00-crore-up-0-64-y-o-y-4608381.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Titan Company are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 4,435.00 crore in September 2019 up 0.64% from Rs. 4,406.84 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 320.16 crore in September 2019 up 1.84% from Rs. 314.38 crore in September 2018.

        Close
      
EBITDA stands at Rs. 543.87 crore in September 2019 up 10.12% from Rs. 493.90 crore in September 2018.
Titan Company EPS has increased to Rs. 3.61 in September 2019 from Rs. 3.54 in September 2018.
Titan Company shares closed at 1,283.80 on November 05, 2019 (NSE) and has given 18.56% returns over the last 6 months and 48.64% over the last 12 months.
