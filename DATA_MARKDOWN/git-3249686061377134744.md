
# TWICE's 'Dance the Night Away' is bringing up the heat with 200 million views

Published at: **2019-11-04T02:03:00+00:00**

Author: **drifter**

Original: [allkpop](https://www.allkpop.com/article/2019/11/twices-dance-the-night-away-is-bringing-up-the-heat-with-200-million-views)

TWICE now has 200 million views on "Dance the Night Away".
The girls' summer bop was released in July 2018, and it took just a little over a year for the girls to achieve the feat. "Dance the Night Away" now joins the girls' many, many MVs with over 200 million views, including "Fancy", "What is Love?", "Likey", "TT", "Like OOH-AHH", "Heart Shaker", "Yes or Yes", "Cheer Up", "Knock Knock", and "Signal".
