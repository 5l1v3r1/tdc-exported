
# OTA announces the 3rd Monthly Junior Tennis Tournament 2019

Published at: **2019-11-04T18:23:00+00:00**

Author: **Times News Service**

Original: [Times of Oman](https://timesofoman.com/article/2173011/Sports/Tennis/OTA-announces-the-3rd-Monthly-Junior-Tennis-Tournament-2019)

Muscat: Oman Tennis Association will organise the third monthly junior tennis tournament 2019 from November 7 to 9.The association aims to give regular playing opportunity to junior players. Promising players are expected to join and confirm their status.The categories are under-8 Unisex Mini Tennis, 10, 12 , 14, 18 under boys and girls singles. The deadline of entries will be November 5 by 12pm which will be followed by a draw.All registration forms of the tournament are available at OTA office in Al Ghubra Oman Olympic Committee (Spors) building and Sultan Qaboos Sports Complex, Bausher Tennis Court.
