
# 30 years of Parinda: Anil Kapoor and Madhuri Dixit cherish the memories

Published at: **2019-11-03T07:10:42+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/30-years-of-parinda-anil-kapoor-and-madhuri-dixit-cherish-the-memories/articleshow/71875316.cms)

Today, one of the noteworthy films of Bollywood 'Parinda' completed 30 years of its successful release and the lead actors of the film Anil Kapoor and Madhuri Dixit are grateful for being associated with the film and are reliving the memories.
Both Anil and Madhuri took to their respective social media handles and shared a post dedicated to the film and expressed how grateful they feel for being associated with the iconic movie. Anil wrote how he is going to relive the memories while Madhuri expressed how thankful and happy she is to work with Anil Kapoor, Jackie Shroff and others for the movie and also felt grateful for portraying a powerful role back in those days.
