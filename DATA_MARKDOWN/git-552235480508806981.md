
# M77 reopen after emergency services rush to reports of 'man falling from bridge'

Published at: **2019-11-04T11:16:24+00:00**

Author: **Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/news/scottish-news/police-incident-closes-m77-northbound-20807704)

A number of emergency services were deployed to the M77 in Glasgow after reports that a person had fallen from a bridge.
Police were called to junction one at Dumbreck at around 10am on Monday, November 4.
The busy motorway was closed northbound as officers and medical staff remained at the scene.
Police reopened the section of the motorway at around 11am.
It is unclear if anyone has been injured in the incident.
 
A spokesperson for Police Scotland said: “At around 10am on Monday, November 4, we received a report that a person had fallen from a bridge at junction one on the M77.
“Junction one northbound was closed while emergency service remain on the scene.”
