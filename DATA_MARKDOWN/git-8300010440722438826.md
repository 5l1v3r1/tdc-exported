
# Mt. San Jacinto College, 3 other schools placed on lockdown after man with gun seen on campus

Published at: **2019-11-06T22:04:45+00:00**

Author: **ABC7.com staff**

Original: [ABC7 Los Angeles](https://abc7.com/lockdown-lifted-at-mt-san-jacinto-college-after-man-with-gun-seen/5676351/)

SAN JACINTO, Calif. (KABC) -- Mt. San Jacinto College and three other nearby schools were placed on lockdown for hours Wednesday after a man with a gun was seen on the college campus, officials said."No shots were fired," an alert on the college's website said.In a tweet, the Riverside County Sheriff's Department said it was notified about the armed man shortly before 9:30 a.m. The agency described the situation as "fluid" and noted that no injuries were reported.
