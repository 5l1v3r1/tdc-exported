
# ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ trailer: Fans left smitten by Kartik Aaryan’s charm and comic timing

Published at: **2019-11-04T14:26:01+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/pati-patni-aur-woh-trailer-fans-left-smitten-by-kartik-aaryans-charm-and-comic-timing/articleshow/71900811.cms)

The trailer of the film 'Pati Patni or Woh' starring Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday is out and it has enough to make the wait for December 6, seem excruciatingly long.
The film features Kartik playing the role of 'Chintu Tyagi' alongside Bhumi Pednekar, who plays the role of his wife while Ananya Panday will be seen in the role of his secretary and girlfriend.
The rib-tickling trailer with its perfect comic timing shows how Chintu tussles to maintain a balance between his married life and an extra-marital affair. Not only had the clip given fans enough content to laugh out loud, but the leading man has also managed to woo the ladies over with his effortless charm and charisma.
From posting his many expressions to gushing about how perfect he is for the part, fans made sure that the hunk featured on the top trends on social media.
