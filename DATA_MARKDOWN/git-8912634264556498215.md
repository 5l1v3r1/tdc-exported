
# Saudi ambassador to Britain calls on tourists from around the world to visit the Kingdom

Published at: **2019-11-07T15:32:48+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Arab News](https://www.arabnews.com/node/1580616/saudi-arabia)

RIYADH: Saudi Arabia’s King Salman hosted the Central Intelligence Agency’s director Gina Haspel on Thursday. The king and Haspel “discussed a number of topics of mutual interest,” Saudi Press Agency reported. The meeting in the capital was attended by a number of Saudi officials including Foreign Minister Prince Faisal bin Farhan and intelligence chief Khalid Al-Humaidan.
