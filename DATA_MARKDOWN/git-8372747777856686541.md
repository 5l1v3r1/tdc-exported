
# Family and dog safe after house fire in Clewiston

Published at: **2019-11-05T14:23:58+00:00**

Author: **WFTX Digital Team**

Original: [Fox 4](https://www.fox4now.com/news/local-news/family-and-dog-safe-after-house-fire-in-clewiston)

HENDRY COUNTY, Fla. -- All residents and pets have been reported safe in a house fire in Clewiston Monday night.
According to the Hendry County Sheriff's Office, the fire was extinguished shortly after 9 p.m. at a home on Nalls Court, off Davidson Road.
Firefighters were able to safely rescue a small dog from the home.
The Red Cross says they responded to assist seven residents with emergency food and lodging needs.
