
# Torrent Power Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 3,776.53 crore, up 11.69% Y-o-Y

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/torrent-power-standalone-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-3776-53-crore-up-11-69-y-o-y-4608371.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Torrent Power are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 3,776.53 crore in September 2019 up 11.69% from Rs. 3,381.34 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 743.71 crore in September 2019 up 84.97% from Rs. 402.08 crore in September 2018.

Torrent Power shares closed at 280.10 on November 05, 2019 (NSE) and has given 10.82% returns over the last 6 months and 5.62% over the last 12 months.
