
# Three protesters killed in Iraq’s Karbala near Iran consulate

Published at: **2019-11-03T20:47:51+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Arab News](https://www.arabnews.com/node/1578446/middle-east)

BAGHDAD: Dozens of Iraqi demonstrators stormed the Iranian consulate in Karbala on Sunday, lowering the flag and burning part of the outer wall.
The incident followed another day of widespread demonstrations in Baghdad and the country's south.
Staff at the consulate escaped through the back door, sources told Arab News.
