
# Gardaí arrest man after boy found dead in Limerick

Published at: **2019-11-03T21:56:00+00:00**

Author: **By David Raleigh**

Original: [Breaking News](https://www.breakingnews.ie/ireland/gardai-arrest-man-after-boy-found-dead-in-limerick-961606.html)

A major garda investigation is underway after the body of a boy was discovered in a house in Limerick city tonight.
It is believed that the boy, said to be aged 11, was found with multiple injuries.
Gardaí are treating his death as suspicious and are at the scene on the Shanabooly Road, Ballynanty.
A man in his late 20s has been arrested by gardaí in connection with the investigation.
