
# Fitbit reports a 12per cent fall in revenue

Published at: **2019-11-07T05:26:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [CNA](https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/business/fitbit-reports-a-12per-cent-fall-in-revenue-12070782)

Wearable device maker Fitbit Inc reported its first drop in revenue in five quarters on Wednesday as it faced intense competition from deeper-pocket companies such as Apple Inc and Samsung Electronics Co Ltd , underscoring challenges for buyer Alphabet Inc's Google.
REUTERS: Wearable device maker Fitbit Inc reported its first drop in revenue in five quarters on Wednesday as it faced intense competition from deeper-pocket companies such as Apple Inc and Samsung Electronics Co Ltd , underscoring challenges for buyer Alphabet Inc's Google.
The company's net loss widened to US$51.9 million, or 20 cents per share, in the third quarter ended Sept. 28, from US$2.1 million, or 1 cent per share, a year earlier.
Revenue fell 11.7per cent to US$347.2 million.
