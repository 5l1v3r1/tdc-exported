
# Chippa Star & Two Others Fly Out!   

Published at: **2019-11-07T19:45:00+00:00**

Author: **Soccer Laduma**

Original: [Soccer Laduma](https://www.soccerladuma.co.za/news/articles/local/categories/south-africa/south-africa-s-u23-team-boosted-by-arrival-of-three-players/666979)

After missing the match against Kaizer Chiefs, Chippa United’s Tercious Malepe flew out to Egypt to join South Africa’s national Under-23 team.
Malepe was red-carded in the Telkom Knockout quarter-final against Mamelodi Sundowns at the weekend, ruling him out for Chiefs and AmaZulu matches respectively.

After leaving Mzansi with only 12 players, coach David Notoane will welcome the addition of Malepe, who is the team's captain, as well as Mondli Mpoto and Keletso Makgalwa who are the latest players to join the team in their preparation for the 2019 U23 Africa Cup of Nations to take place in Egypt from 8-22 November.

The three players arrived in Cairo this morning. Notoane’s side will open their AFCON campaign with a match against Zambia on Saturday.
Meanwhile, Notoane's side lost 2-0 to Milutin Sredojevic’s Zamalek in a practice match played on Wednesday afternoon.
