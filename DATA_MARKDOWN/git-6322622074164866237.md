
# StarHub reports 1.7% rise in 3Q earnings to S$58 mil despite revenue dip

Published at: **2019-11-05T17:38:07+00:00**

Author: **Benjamin Cher**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/starhub-reports-17-rise-3q-earnings-s58-mil-despite-revenue-dip)

SINGAPORE (Nov 5): StarHub saw its earnings rise slightly to S$58.0 million for the 3QFY19 ended September, from S$57.0 million a year ago.
However, the group reported a 1.6% dip in revenue to S$572.6 million during the quarter, from S$582.2 million a year ago.
The decline was mainly due to lower revenues from its Mobile, Pay TV and Broadband businesses.
