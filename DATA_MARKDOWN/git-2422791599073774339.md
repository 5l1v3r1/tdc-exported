
# Amber Alert cancelled after Ontario teenagers found safe

Published at: **2019-11-07T04:52:00+00:00**

Author: **Brooklyn Neustaeter, CTVNews.ca Overnight Producer**

Original: [www.ctvnews.ca](https://www.ctvnews.ca/canada/amber-alert-cancelled-after-ontario-teenagers-found-safe-1.4673855)

TORONTO -- Authorities in Ontario say two teenagers who were the subject of an Amber Alert have been found safe.
Police say the 13-year-old and 15-year-old were located after they were last seen in Toronto's west end Wednesday morning. They were believed to have been abducted by their father.
An Amber Alert was issued by the Ontario Provincial Police at the request of Toronto police at approximately 11:30 p.m. on Wednesday night. It was cancelled about an hour later.
Police say a suspect is in custody in connection to the Amber Alert.
