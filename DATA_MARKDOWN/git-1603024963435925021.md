
# Cochin Minerals Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 57.21 crore, up 55.6% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-07T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/cochin-minerals-standalone-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-57-21-crore-up-55-6-y-o-y-4614061.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Cochin Minerals and Rutile are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 57.21 crore in September 2019 up 55.6% from Rs. 36.77 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 2.38 crore in September 2019 up 43.08% from Rs. 1.67 crore in September 2018.
