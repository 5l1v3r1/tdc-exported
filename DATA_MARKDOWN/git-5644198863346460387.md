
# Olympique Lyonnais' shares rise after Benfica Champions League win

Published at: **2019-11-06T16:28:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [CNA](https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/sport/olympique-lyonnais--shares-rise-after-benfica-champions-league-win-12068928)

REUTERS: Shares in French soccer club Olympique Lyonnais rose on Wednesday after Lyon revived their hopes of reaching the Champions League last 16 with a victory over Benfica.
Lyon's shares were up around 1per cent in early trading.
Lyon beat Benfica 3-1 on Tuesday evening.
