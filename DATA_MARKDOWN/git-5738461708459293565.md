
# Louth LGFA announce new senior football team manager

Published at: **2019-11-01T20:03:00+00:00**

Author: **Caoimhín Reilly, at Páirc Clan na Gael**

Original: [Dundalk Democrat](https://www.dundalkdemocrat.ie/news/home/490782/louth-lgfa-announce-new-senior-football-team-manager.html)


        Wayne Freeman has been ratified as Louth LGFA's new senior team manager at tonight's County Board meeting at Páirc Clan na Gael.
      
The Kildare man, who hails from Monasterevin, replaces Darren Bishop, who stood down following September's All-Ireland JFC title win.
