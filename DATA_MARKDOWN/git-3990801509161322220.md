
# Tesla’s ‘Blade Runner’ pickup truck will be revealed November 21

Published at: **2019-11-06T20:08:24+00:00**

Author: **Napier Lopez**

Original: [Plugged | The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/plugged/2019/11/06/teslas-blade-runner-pickup-truck-will-be-revealed-november-21/?utm_campaign=tweet6)

We finally know when we’ll learn more about Tesla‘s pickup truck: November 21. The ‘Cybertruck,’ as Musk has previously dubbed it, is said to look like something straight out of Blade Runner. Indeed, Musk made his announcement with an allusion to the original film’s opening.
