
# Essex crash: One dead and three injured after car ploughs into pub

Published at: **2019-11-03T03:44:38+00:00**

Author: **Elaine McCahill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/elaine-mccahill/**

Original: [mirror](https://www.mirror.co.uk/news/uk-news/breaking-essex-crash-one-dead-20800564)

One person has died after a car drove into a pub in Essex in the early hours of this morning.
The victim is believed to be a man iin his 40s while three people have been taken to hospital, one with life-changing injuries, Essex Police said.
A man has been arrested on suspicion of murder following the fatal collision.
Police were called to the Spinnaker Public House in Hythe Quay, Colchester, to reports of a car hitting the pub shortly after 12.30am on Sunday.

The arrested man has also been detained on suspicion of grievous bodily harm, death by dangerous driving and assault.
Essex Police tweeted: "We are on scene of a serious collision at The Spinnaker Public House, Hythe Quay, #Colchester.
"The road is closed and is likely to be shut into the morning, drivers are being advised to avoid the area."
Pictures from the scene show numerous emergency services outside the pub.
Anyone with information is asked to call 101.
