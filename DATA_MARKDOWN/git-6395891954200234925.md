
# Vera Clemente, widow of Hall of Famer Roberto Clemente in 'delicate health'

Published at: **2019-11-02T15:23:37+00:00**

Author: **WPXI.com**

Original: [WFXT](https://www.boston25news.com/news/trending-now/vera-clemente-widow-of-hall-of-famer-roberto-clemente-in-delicate-health-/1004338662?utm_source=homestream&utm_medium=site_navigation&utm_campaign=homestream_click)

The 81-year-old widow of Pittsburgh Pirates Hall of Famer Roberto Clemente reportedly has been hospitalized and is in poor health.
According to a tweet from the Pirates, Vera Clemente "is in delicate health."
