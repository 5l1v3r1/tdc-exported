
# FEL Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 1,323.19 crore, up 9.02% Y-o-Y

Published at: **2019-11-06T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/earnings/fel-standalone-september-2019-net-sales-at-rs-1323-19-crore-up-9-02-y-o-y-4608401.html)


        Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Future Enterprises Limited are:
      
Net Sales at Rs 1,323.19 crore in September 2019 up 9.02% from Rs. 1,213.74 crore in September 2018.
Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 16.22 crore in September 2019 up 132.05% from Rs. 6.99 crore in September 2018.

        Close
      
EBITDA stands at Rs. 398.81 crore in September 2019 up 17.13% from Rs. 340.48 crore in September 2018.
FEL EPS has increased to Rs. 0.33 in September 2019 from Rs. 0.19 in September 2018.
FEL shares closed at 26.30 on November 05, 2019 (NSE) and has given -20.42% returns over the last 6 months and -33.16% over the last 12 months.
