
# Issues between Govt, JUI-F should be resolved soon: Pervaiz Elahi

Published at: **2019-11-05T06:37:00+00:00**

Author: **https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews**

Original: [Dunya News](https://dunyanews.tv/en/Pakistan/517239-Issues-between-Govt,-JUI-F-should-be-resolved-soon:-Pervaiz-Elahi)

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Pakistan Muslim League-Quaid (PML-Q) chief Chaudhry Shujaat Hussain and Punjab Assembly Speaker Chaudhry Pervaiz Elahi called on Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam-Fazl (JUI-F) chief Maulana Fazlur Rehman on Monday night at his residence and discussed JUI-F’s sit-in and Azadi March, Dunya News reported.
Opposition’s Rahbar Committee head Akram Durrani and Chaudhry Pervaiz Elahi talked to media after the meeting.
Akram Durrani said that talks with government’s negotiation committee and Azadi March came under discussion during the meeting and we appreciate Chaudhry brother efforts in this regard.
Speaking on the occasion, Chaudhry Pervaiz Elahi said that issues between government and JUI-F should be resolved soon. He said that we don’t want creation of issues due to current political situation in the country.
