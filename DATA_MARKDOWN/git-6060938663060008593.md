
# Photos: Sara Ali Khan goes on a shopping spree with her mom Amrita Singh

Sara Ali Khan often gets snapped in the city on various occasions. Be it her visit to salon sessions or movie outings, the actress always makes sure to flash her sweet smile for the cameras.
And today, she was clicked with her mom Amrita Singh as they stepped out for some shopping together. Sara was spotted while leaving an apparel store. The young starlet looked happy on her day out with her mommy!
Sara was sporting an off-shoulder top paired with gym shorts and you can't miss that glee on her face. Amrita, on the other hand, was in a blue kurta teamed with white pyjama and flip flops.
