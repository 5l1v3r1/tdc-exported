
# Orange County gang task force makes several arrests in Santa Ana

Published at: **2019-11-07T21:01:11+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [ABC7 Los Angeles](https://abc7.com/oc-gang-task-force-makes-several-arrests-in-santa-ana/5678444/)

SANTA ANA, Calif. (KABC) -- The FBI's Orange County violent gang task force executed several arrest warrants in Santa Ana and other cities Thursday, according to officials.Authorities say 11 people were indicted in an operation that uncovered guns, ammunition, money and drugs.Some of the suspects were also arrested for violent crimes, including murder and attempted murder.Officials were expected to announce additional details at a press conference at the Orange County district attorney's office.An investigation is ongoing.DEVELOPING: This story will be updated as more information becomes available.
