
# Death investigation under way near heavy machinery in Fort Myers

Published at: **2019-11-06T14:01:08+00:00**

Author: **WFTX Digital Team**

Original: [Fox 4](https://www.fox4now.com/news/local-news/death-investigation-under-way-near-heavy-machinery-in-fort-myers)

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- Lee County Sheriff's deputies responded to the scene of a death investigation off Alico Road Wednesday.
The scene is on Indy Drive. That's north of Alico Road and east of Michael G. Rippe Parkway.
The investigation is taking place near large heavy machinery on what appears to be a construction site.
No further information is available at this time. We'll update as soon as we learn more.
