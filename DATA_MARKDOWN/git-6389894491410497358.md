
# Philip Hammond to step down as MP at December election

Published at: **2019-11-05T15:38:16+00:00**

Author: **Joe Curtis**

Original: [City A.M.](https://www.cityam.com/philip-hammond-to-step-down-as-mp-at-december-election/)

Philip Hammond will quit politics at the upcoming general election, the former Treasury boss confirmed today.
Hammond, who has railed against Boris Johnson’s Brexit strategy from the backbenches since leaving his post as chancellor, said he has made the decision with “great sadness”.
