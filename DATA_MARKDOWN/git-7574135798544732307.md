
# Tame Impala announce North American tour dates with Clairo, MGMT

Published at: **2019-11-01T10:58:00+00:00**

Author: **Bill Pearis**

Original: [BrooklynVegan](http://www.brooklynvegan.com/tame-impala-announce-north-american-tour-dates-with-clairo-mgmt/)

Tame Impala‘s new album The Slow Rush will be out on Valentine’s Day 2020 and the band have just announced their first North American shows in support of it. So far, only announced dates are on the West Coast with Clairo: San Diego’s Pechanga Arena on March 9, Los Angeles’ The Forum on March 10 and San Francisco’s Chase Center on March 13. They’ll also play Mexico City’s Foro Sol on March 19, which has MGMT on the bill, too. Tickets for the U.S. dates go on sale Saturday, November 9 at noon Pacific, and the Mexico City show is on sale Friday, Nov 8 at 11:00 AM local time. Tickets come with a digital or CD copy of The Slow Rush.
