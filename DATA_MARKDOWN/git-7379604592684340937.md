
# Boy, 11, killed after being knocked off his bike in Warwickshire village as man arrested

Published at: **2019-11-03T12:31:33+00:00**

Author: **Patrick Knox**

Original: [The Sun](https://www.thesun.co.uk/news/10269794/boy-killed-bike-crash-nuneaton-police/)


        A MAN has been arrested after an 11-year-old boy was killed while riding his bike in a Warwickshire village.
      
Police were called to the crash in a country road in Ansley Comon near Nuneaton at 2.40pm yesterday.
Paramedics battled to save the youngster and rushed him to hospital where he was pronounced dead.
A Warwickshire Police spokesman said: “This is a tragic incident and we’re appealing to anyone who may have witnessed what happened or was passing with dash cameras in this location to please contact us.
“Our thoughts are with the boy’s family at this time.”
The arrested man has been released under investifation.
Anyone with information should call 101 quoting incident number 215 of 2 November 2019.
