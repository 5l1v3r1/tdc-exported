
# Ohio detective in 'grave' condition after being shot in face twice during drug raid, 4 men in custody

Published at: **2019-11-05T22:56:26+00:00**

Author: **Talia Kaplan**

Original: [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/us/ohio-detective-in-grave-condition-after-being-shot-in-face-twice-during-drug-raid-police-say)

An Ohio detective is on life support in “grave” condition after being shot while serving a drug-related warrant, authorities said on Tuesday.
Dayton police Chief Richard Biehl told reporters that Jorge DelRio, a 30 year veteran of the department, was shot twice in the face on Monday while working with a Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) task force.
